    Lando Norris’ girlfriend Luisinha Barosa Oliveira shared a stunning throwback picture in a pink bikini to her 280,000 Instagram on Monday. Oliveira uploaded the photo along with the caption: “A little throwback with @kanawaswim. A super comfortable bikini, and like the rest of all them, the fit is amazing.”

    The snap was unsurprisingly well received by her followers, with the post racking up 65,000 likes and 134 comments in the space of one day. Since then, the Portuguese model has treated her fans to another Instagram post, after uploading an album of snaps of her alongside Norris at last weekend’s Monaco Grand Prix.

    Norris put in a solid performance in front of his partner, finishing the race in sixth to leave him in seventh spot in the 2022 driver standings, one place behind Lewis Hamilton.

Their relationship confirmation took social media by storm, with the post receiving a remarkable 1.4 million likes and nearly 20,000 comments.

    One of those 20,000 came from fellow F1 driver and former McLaren teammate Carlos Sainz, who commented: “It’s official.” It has been reported that Norris and Oliveira had been dating for over a year, before finally going public with their relationship in January 2022.

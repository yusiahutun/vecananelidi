    What Is a Rockstar Social Club Nickname?

    Rockstar Social Club is a digital rights management, multiplayer, and communications service developed by Rockstar for use across all of its titles. Creating an account with them allows you to use features on GTA Online such as Crews, and Snapmatic Photo Sharing.

    When you create an account with Rockstar Social Club, it'll ask you to come up with a nickname. This nickname is what will identify you on Rockstar games such as GTA Online and Red Dead Online.

    It's worth mentioning there is also a Rockstar Games Launcher, which offers a free copy of GTA San Andreas.

    Changing Your Rockstar Social Club Nickname

    If you want to change your Rockstar Social Club nickname, you just need to follow these simple steps. You should keep in mind that you can only change your nickname once per year, so if you're going to change make sure that it's to something you'll be happy with for at least the next twelve months.

  • Log into Social Club.
  • Click your profile in the upper right, then select Settings.
  • On the Profile tab, click Edit next to Nickname.
  • Enter your new desired nickname.
  • Click Save Changes.

    • You should now have your new Rockstar Social Club nickname for use on GTA Online and Red Dead Online.

    Your New Rockstar Name

    Having a name that you want to represent you is great for building your online gaming identity, and by following the above steps you should now have a name to take with you on GTA Online and Red Dead Online that suits you.

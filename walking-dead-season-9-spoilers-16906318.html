    The Walking Dead star teases saucy shower scene amid Daryl and Connie romance

    03 Mar 2024, 00:00

    Since The Walking Dead returned from its mid-season hiatus, fans have been hoping for a romance between Daryl and Connie.

    They've been working closely the past few episodes and fans are hoping their relationship could turn romantic.

    Now, actress Lauren Ridloff, who plays Connie, has hinted there could be something saucy in the characters' futures.

    Speaking via an interpreter on Talking Dead, Lauren said: "Well, maybe there will be a shower scene. I think they both need that."

    The star continued: "I think seriously, honestly, I think right now it's definitely much more like Butch Cassidy and the Sundance Kid kind of pairing. I think both of them are still great judges of people.

    "They don't say much, first off, but they have so much in common, which makes sense for them to work together. And then maybe the shower scene will come."

    Seeing Connie is deaf, just like Lauren, she and Daryl often communicate with pen and paper, though Connie also reads lips.

    And despite her disadvantages, Connie has proved to be a skilled fighter, fending off enemies with her trusted slingshot.

    She took out a handful of Whisperers in this weeks episode after rescuing Henry (Matt Lintz) and Lydia (Cassady McClincy), alongside Daryl (Norman Reedus).

    Things looked like they were going downhill when Henry was stabbed, but his injuries were soon tended to.

    A fight scene between Daryl and Beta (Ryan Hurst) soon followed, and it looked like Daryl could have met his match.

    But, knowing the location better, Daryl hid before ambushing Beta and pushing him down an open elevator shaft.

    What he doesn't know though, is that Beta survived the fall and is now out for blood even more.

    The Walking Dead continues Mondays at 9pm on Fox UK and streams on NOW TV.

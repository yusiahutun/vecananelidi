    When searching for jobs on LinkedIn, you might find an opportunity you want to apply for later. But what if you forget the job posting or the keyword that led you to it?

    To avoid this, you can save jobs on LinkedIn so that you can always come back to them. But how do you save jobs on LinkedIn, and how do you find them later?

    How to Save a Job on LinkedIn

    LinkedIn's Saved Jobs feature makes it easy to find and manage jobs you're interested in. This feature saves you time by eliminating the need to remember the keywords that led you to your desired job.

    Saving a job search on LinkedIn is easy, but the method is different for mobile and desktop devices. Let's take a look at how to do it on both.

    If you're using the LinkedIn website, follow these steps:

  • Launch LinkedIn, and click Jobs in the top bar.
  • Type your preferred job title and location in their respective field and press Enter.
  • Click on the job of your liking and click Save in the right window.

    • That's it. You've successfully saved a job.

    If you're using the LinkedIn mobile app, follow these steps instead:

    Close
  • Open LinkedIn and tap Jobs in the bottom right corner.
  • Search for the job opportunities you want to view.
  • Open the job you want to save from the displayed list.
  • Tap Save.

    • Now that you've saved jobs on LinkedIn, let's check out how to view them.

    How to Find a Saved Job on LinkedIn

    To find the job you saved on LinkedIn, you need to go to the dedicated My Jobs menu.

    To find this menu using the LinkedIn website, follow these steps:

  • Open LinkedIn, and click Jobs in the top bar.
  • Select My jobs in the left sidebar.

    • You can find all your saved jobs in the Saved Jobs section. From there, you can click on the three dots next to any job to perform actions such as applying for the job, copying the link, or removing a job from the saved list.

    If you're using the mobile app, follow these steps to find a saved job:

    Close
  • Launch LinkedIn, tap your profile picture in the top right corner, and choose Viewprofile.
  • Select Show all resources.
  • Tap on My items.
  • Then select My Jobs to view all the saved jobs.

    • To apply for the job, select the three dots next to the saved job and choose Apply. You can also select Unsave from the three dots menu if you are no longer interested in that job.

    But before you send your application, make sure you've followed the right steps before applying for an online job.

    Manage Saved Jobs on LinkedIn

    It's common to lose track of jobs you're interested in. To avoid this, you can follow the above steps to quickly save jobs you like on LinkedIn. This way, you can apply for them whenever you have time.

    This Christmas must have been extra special for Norman Reedus and Diane Kruger! The doting dad-of-two was able to spend the holiday with his and Diane’s new baby girl — that they welcomed together in early November — and, to celebrate his daughter’s first Xmas, Norman shared the first photo of his little girl via Instagram on Christmas Eve.

    “A lot to be thankful for this year, this one especially,” he captioned the sweet photo of himself holding the baby’s tiny hand. Norman, 49, also gave a shout-out to his 19-year-old son, Mingus Reedus, whom he had with his ex-girlfriend Helena Christensen. “Happy family,” he wrote before adding Helena’s Instagram handle.

    Once Diane, 42, saw Norman’s sweet Instagram post, she wrote him a cute comment below the photo. “I love you,” she wrote to which Norman responded, “@dianekruger you n me.” How romantic! Diane and Norman have been dating since 2016 and still have yet to disclose the name of their daughter to the public.

    As fans eagerly wait to learn the moniker of Diane and Kruger’s new child, Diane gushed to E! News about the “great moment of joy” that she experienced this Christmas with the baby. “We have a lot to be thankful for. It’s been a wonderful year,” she recently said. “I’m just looking forward to having my own little family be together, have take-out because I don’t think I can step away to cook!”

    So far, Diane has described motherhood to be a “very rewarding experience” in a recent interview with Extra. Though she loves being a mom, her daughter has made her very “tired” but it’s worth it because it makes her feel like a “superhero.” Awww!

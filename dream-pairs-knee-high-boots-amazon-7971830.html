    As a shopping editor, it should come as no surprise that I love to shop. I rarely need an excuse to do so, but the seasonal shift has me working overtime, searching for the best fall pieces. And so far, my current hyperfixation has been on finding the cutest and the comfiest knee-high boots. My ideal pair would come in a classic black, so they are easy to style — and if first impressions go well, I’d want them in a variety of other shades, too. They should also have a sturdy heel that provides ample support and comfort and some elasticity around the calf to prevent the shoes from digging and rubbing. And according to the 3,200-plus five-star ratings, Dream Pairs Knee High Boots are all of the above.

    Dream Pairs boots come in faux leather and suede styles in black, white, burgundy, gray, blue, and khaki colorways. They’re available in sizes 5 through 11, and feature a 2.5-inch block heel, a side zipper closure for easy on and off, and a slightly padded inner faux fur lining. 

    Dream Pairs Knee-High Boots

    While the boots' classic design initially drew me in, it’s the over 700 five-star reviews that convinced me these knee-high boots belong in my fall rotation. One flight attendant said they wore the boots for up to 15 hours, and while they needed some breaking in, they were “comfortable,” and they’re planning on picking up a second pair. 

    Another reviewer who works at a bar said the heels were so comfortable they felt like they were “wearing flats.” They said the heel wasn’t “high enough that your feet [would] hurt if you [wore] them for a few hours standing” and that the faux suede is the “softest” they’ve ever felt. They also mentioned that they didn’t have to keep pulling up the boot because they “gently hug” around the calf.

    A final shopper said these were the “best boots ever” and that their color goes with everything, making them easy to style. They also said they fit “true-to-size,” “have some stretch for added comfort,” and when they spilled coffee on them, they didn’t get stained and were easy to clean.

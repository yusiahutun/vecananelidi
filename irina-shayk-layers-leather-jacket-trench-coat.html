    Irina Shayk Layers a Distressed Leather Jacket over a Long Trench Coat

    Irina Shayk expertly layered two very different coats while out and about during Paris Fashion Week. The modelwho has been busy this past month walking runways in Milan, New York, and Parisshowed off her off-duty style while in between shows yesterday.

    01 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Irina Shayk expertly layered two very different coats while out and about during Paris Fashion Week.

    The model—who has been busy this past month walking runways in Milan, New York, and Paris—showed off her off-duty style while in between shows yesterday.

    Shayk opted for unconventional layering by wearing an oversized distressed leather biker jacket over a longer beige trench coat. Both outerwear options hail from H&M Studio's spring/summer 2023 collection.

    Underneath the draping trench coat, Shayk wore a black base consisting of a turtleneck and short shorts with a raw hem. She capped the look off with ribbed white mid-calf socks, black square-toe boots, and large silver hoop earrings.

    There have been plenty of off-duty sightings of Shayk this season.

    Last week, she was spotted wearing a monochromatic slate gray all-leather ensemble, consisting of a jacket and matching trousers, outside of the Tod's fashion show in Milan.

    Before that, she pulled off a nearly incognito all-black look in New York City, composed of a long black blazer coat layered over a black hoodie, plus pleated black trousers, leather dress shoes, a puffy black leather tote bag, and oversized shield sunglasses.

    Raymond Hall//Getty Images

    In conversation with Michael Kors for Harper's BAZAAR's September 2021 issue, Shayk opened up about getting dressed as the mom of five-year-old daughter Lea De Seine, whom she shares with ex Bradley Cooper.

    "She looks at me and she goes, 'Mm-mm … Take it off,'" Shayk said, referring to Lea's reaction to her wardrobe. "In fact, for my birthday in January, I was in Florida and I wore this fluffy dress. I never celebrate my birthday, but I was like, okay, I'm going to put on a nice dress, couple friends came. I put on this beautiful black dress, kind of fluffy. And my daughter's face—she looks at me and she goes, 'Take it off.' I say, 'Why? Mama doesn't look pretty?' She said, 'No. It's too beautiful.' So she doesn't want me to be more beautiful than her because her dress is not fluffy. It's not a competition! I was like, no. And I explained to her, I was like, 'Everyone is beautiful in their own way.' But she already has this. I was like, 'That's definitely not from me.'"

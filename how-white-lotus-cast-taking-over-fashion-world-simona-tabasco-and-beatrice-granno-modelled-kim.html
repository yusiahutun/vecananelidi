    How The White Lotus cast is taking over the fashion world: Simona Tabasco and Beatrice Grann model

    The reel and real-life besties who played double-trouble act Mia and Lucia in the show heated things up in Kardashians limited edition lace pink and black lingerie sets from the brands Fits Everybody and Silk collections.

    27 Apr 2024, 00:00
    Business mogul Kim Kardashian might be the biggest fan of the show’s breakout stars. Skims recently dropped its Valentine’s Day campaign starring Tabasco, 28, and Grannò, 29.

    The reel and real-life besties – who played double-trouble act Mia and Lucia in the show – heated things up in Kardashian’s limited edition lace pink and black lingerie sets from the brand’s Fits Everybody and Silk collections.

    “I watched The White Lotus and had to have my girls!” Kardashian wrote in her Instagram caption.

    Sabrina Impacciatore and Simona Tabasco at J.W. Anderson’s show

    During the Menswear Fashion Week in Milan, Italy, 54-year-old Impacciatore took a break from her character Valentina’s hotel manager suits and put on a vibrant J.W. Anderson ensemble instead – a multicoloured jumpsuit with an overall print of a tropical sunset and a pair of sleek patent leather boots.

    7 handbags named after famous female icons, from Princess Diana to Selena Gomez

    Tabasco also attended the pre-autumn 2023 fashion show and sparkled in a black backless dress designed by Jonathan Anderson, as reported by Grazia.

    Jennifer Coolidge won the Golden Globes in Dolce & Gabbana

    Channelling her beloved TV character Tanya’s innate glitz and glamour, 61-year-old Coolidge donned a black custom sequinned Dolce & Gabbana gown as she received the best supporting actress in a limited series award.

    Adam DiMarco attended Dior and Prada’s shows

    The White Lotus’ male cast has been dominating the menswear fashion world, too! Resident “good boy” Adam DiMarco turned heads at Dior and Prada’s presentations. Sitting front row, the Canadian stud sported a Kim Jones-designed pink velour tracksuit with matching Dior trainers.

    DiMarco also wore a head-to-toe Prada polo neck and trousers and capped it off with a black-and-white gingham coat.

    Theo James and Will Sharpe attended Armani

    Fictional tech bros James and Sharpe looked dapper at Milan Menswear Fashion Week. The Divergent actor attended the Giorgio Armani fashion show in a navy blue double-breasted suit and a pair of shiny black boots.

    The Kardashian-Jenner clan’s most expensive Hermès handbags, ranked

    Sharpe, meanwhile, traded his character Ethan’s workout wear for a checked grey coat layered over matching denim at the Emporio Armani presentation. He was also spotted at the Loewe Menswear autumn 2023 show with season 1 alum Murray Bartlett.

    Aubrey Plaza and Meghann Fahy in Louis Vuitton

    Plaza and Fahy’s TV characters, Harper and Daphne, could not be more different. In real life though, the talented actresses do seem to have similar tastes. At the Critics’ Choice Awards, Plaza dominated the red carpet in a Louis Vuitton black sequinned dress with ombre yellow and red tulle detailing.

    Fahy reunited with her on-screen husband, Theo James, at the AFI Awards luncheon. She rocked an all-black style in a Louis Vuitton minidress and a pair of knee-high leather boots.

    Simona Tabasco wore Giambattista Valli

    Tabasco proved she’s the current “It” girl by keeping the fun going at Paris Haute Couture Fashion Week 2023. She was dressed in a tangerine tulle dress with woven gold trim and criss-cross detailing.

    “My dress reminds me of a sunset,” Tabasco told Vogue. “It was love at first sight – it makes me feel like a positive energy bearer with all of its colours.”

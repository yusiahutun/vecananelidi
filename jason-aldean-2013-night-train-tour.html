    29 Sep 2024, 00:00

    All aboard! Jason Aldean has announced cities for the first leg of his 2013 Night Train Tour, which will kick off Feb. 21 in Bossier City, La. and roll through in 23 cities before moving on to the next leg.

    Aldean fans will have ample opportunities to see the 'The Only Way I Know' hitmaker in concert -- and he won't be alone. The singer will be taking country crooners Jake Owen and Thomas Rhett aboard his train, as well.

    "The thing I'm most excited about for next year is getting to add some new songs off the 'Night Train' album into my show," Aldean said in a statement. "As a musician, it's great to be able to shake up the set list and think of cool ways to present these new songs in the live show. We're already working on some cool production ideas, and we're going to come back from the holidays ready to go. I'm a big fan of both Jake and Thomas Rhett, so it'll be awesome to have those guys out with us."

    The Night Train Tour has already been making waves in the news. When Aldean announced the tour's stadium dates, tickets flew out of the box office like hotcakes. In just minutes, the popular country star had sold out his shows at the University of Georgia’s Sanford Stadium in Athens, Ga., Boston’s Fenway Park and Wrigley Field in Chicago.

    Specific dates for the first leg of the tour will be announced soon.

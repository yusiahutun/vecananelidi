    Claudia Jordan is an American actress who is known for appearing as a model on the U.S. version of the “Deal or No Deal” and “The Price Is Right”. She is also known for competing in the season 2 and 6 of the “Celebrity Apprentice.” She also appeared in the seventh season of the Bravo reality series “The Real Housewives of Atlanta.”

    Early Life

    Claudia Angela Jordan, known as Claudia Jordan was born on April 12, 1973, in Providence, Rhode Island in the United States of America. She is the daughter of an Italian mother and an African- American father who met when her father was in the U.S. Air Force stationed at Brindisi, Italy. Claudia Jordan attended a high school in Rhode Island. She then attended and graduated from the Baldwin Wallace University and Long Island University but the field in which she graduated is not yet known.

    Claudia Jordan Career

    Claudia started her acting career by first appearing in the music videos for Backstreet Boys, Ginuwine, Fabolous, Charlie Wilson, Joe, Chico De Barge, D’Angelo, Coolio, Master P, Ludacris, and Kenny Lattimore. She then started appearing in the television commercials for Sears, Pepsi, Visa, Microsoft, and many more. She also appeared as a contestant on Dog Eat Dog before appearing on the Deal or No Deal. Formerly, Jordan was also known as Baker’s Beauty on the CBS Game Show.

    After that, she appeared in season 2 of The Celebrity Apprentice in which the celebrities raise money for a charity of their choice. Jordan was then selected by Donald Trump to co-host the Miss Universe 2009 pageant from the Bahamas along with Billy Bush. Jordan’s first hosting job was for Fox Sports West as a red -carpet correspondent and also appeared on The Best Damn Sports Show Period. She also appeared on the E! Talk Show and VH1. She was also seen on her own morning show in Dallas, The Morning Rush which was the top-rated Rand B Morning show in Dallas. Jordan then started her own talk show on the Fox Soul Platform which is known as Out Loud with Claudia Jordan.

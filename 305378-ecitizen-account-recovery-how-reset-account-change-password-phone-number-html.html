    The Government of Kenya has been aggressive towards the implementation of digital transformation. This targets all the public sectors in a move meant to ensure convenient and effective services to each citizen. To this effect, the eCitizen portal was established and launched to serve as the integrated unit for all services. However, most people are still not familiar with most steps on the portal and are often left to ask ‘How do I recover my eCitizen account?’. Here are the guidelines to go about eCitizen account recovery.

    Your eCitizen portal serves as the one-stop place for you, a Kenyan citizen, to access any government service online. From applying for Certificate of Good Conduct, registering a new company, to even renewing your driving license, all these, as well as other government services, are now at your fingertips. Gone are the days when you had to put up with long queues in different offices within the city.

    However, the convenience comes at a cost. The first one is the convenience fee that you will pay as the portal’s service charge. The other cost is a tragedy that is already too familiar even with other websites; keeping a memory of the passwords. This is one of the key reasons why people opt to change details on eCitizen Kenya. But how do you go about the process?

    Significance of the eCitizen account recovery process to Kenyan citizens

    Understandably, people are usually compelled to keep several online personas. From personal and business emails to social media login details, it is inevitable that you may constantly forget passwords. Fortunately, eCitizen Kenya portal has several measures in place to help users recover fast after the memory shortfall. So how can you reset your eCitizen password?

    How to reset your eCitizen password

    Here are the simple steps to follow whenever you want to reset your account:

  • Visit the eCitizen login page then click on ‘Forgot your password.’ The link is usually located above ‘Log in.’
  • The next page will prompt you to key in your email address (the one you used to sign up for the eCitizen account) or national Identification Number. Once you have entered the details, you can click on the ‘Reset My Password’ button.
  • The next stage will require you to state the method you prefer to use reset and get a new password. This means that you will choose whether to get the reset code via text message to the number you registered with or the login email address for your eCitizen portal. It is better to opt to get the code through text message as it is often faster. After taking your pick, click on continue then proceed to the next stage.
  • Check your email inbox or phone for the confirmation code.
  • Key in the sent code on the reset web page.
  • You will be required to type the new password you will use. Create it then confirm it again.
  • Go back to the sign in page then log in using this new password.

    How do I change phone number on e-Citizen?

    If you need to change your phone number or any other details on eCitizen, first log in to the portal. Next, click on my account tab and your profile will open. Finally, click on phone number and edit it. Using this method, you can also click on the relevant fields on your profile and edit them as needed.

    eCitizen contacts for all your enquiries

    In case you are having problems completing your application for whatever government service, you can always reach out through the following contacts:

    • Email: support@ecitizen.go.ke
    • Phone: +254 780 206 206
    • Immigration contacts: +254 708 206 206 (Mombasa Office) +254 772 246 669 (Nairobi Office)
    • eVisa queries contacts: +254 709 480 000
    • Business registration contacts: +254 709 480 000
    • NTSA contacts: +254 709 480 000

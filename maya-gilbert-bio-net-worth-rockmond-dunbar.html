    Meet Maya Gilbert, Rockmond Dunbar Wife

    Maya Gilbert and Rockmond Dunbar started dating in early 2012, and just within a year, on December 30th, Rockmond proposed to Maya with a ring. Their engagement took place in Montego Bay, Jamaica, and the 10-carat ring was comprised of a 5-carat cushion-cut fancy yellow diamond set in 18k gold accompanied with 21 cushions cut diamonds to complete the 5-carat band.

    The pair then went on to marry a year later and also became first-time parents on December 6 that year. Their firstborn is named Berkeley Seon Dunbar. She was introduced to Rockmond’s fans through his Twitter post captioned, “Berkeley Seon Dunbar 8.3lbs 20.5in…. Mom is doing great. We are absolutely in LOVE with our daughter!”

    Also, the couple went on to welcome their second and third kid named Czar and Pharaoh on February 6, 2016, and July 2017 respectively.

    Their last child Sultan Mosiah Dao Dunbar then arrived in their world on April 1, 2019. Since he was born on April Fool’s day, they labeled him as “Our Nobody’s fool, April Fool’s Baby.”

    The detail about Maya and Rockmond’s wedding is a mystery to us. But earlier in 2013, the 9-1-1 star mentioned that they signed up for a competition that could potentially earn them a celebrity designer wedding at the Empire State Building. So, if all went according to plans, they could have tied the knot in New York.

    According to Maya, her husband and 4 kids were her best friends in this whole world.”

    Maya Gilbert And Eddie Murphy’s Relationship

    Before Rockmond, Maya Gilbert was previously in a relationship with A Thousand Words star Eddie Murphy. The two started dating in March 2010, and just when their relationship was becoming the talk of national TV, they separated 2 months later.

    Since then, Eddie has gone on to date stars like Jasmine Guy, Toni Braxton, Melanie Brown, Tracey Edmonds, Lara LaRue, Whitney Houston, Rocsi Diaz, Nicole Mitchell, and Melody Thornton. Yeah! The list is long.

    As for Maya, she found true love with Rockmond, 2 years after her split with Eddie.

    At the time Eddie was still healing from his ex-wife Ivy Holmes’s act of infidelity. Reportedly, she cheated on the 9-1-1 star with the American athlete Deion Sanders

    Eddie and Ivy were married from 2003 to 2006 and shared no children.

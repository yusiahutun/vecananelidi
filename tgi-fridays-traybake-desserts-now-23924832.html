    Fans of popular chain TGI Friday’s can rejoice, as the famous restaurant and Finsbury Food Group are launching a duo of desserts that are exclusively available at Tesco.

    The desserts come in two delicious sounding flavours that chocolate lovers are bound to be excited by, Chocolate Fudge Fixation and Chocolate Rocky Road.

    The Chocolate Fudge Fixation traybake is made from moist chocolate sponge topped with tasty toffee fudge buttercream swirl and hand decorated with fudge cubes, Belgian milk chocolate and carefully adorned caramel flavour curls.

    Dessert lovers opting for the Chocolate Rocky Road traybake will find the same chocolate sponge but with a Belgian milk chocolate ganache frosting. It comes topped with chocolate coated biscuit balls, marshmallows, chocolate curls and perfectly finished with a white chocolate flavoured drizzle.

    The desserts cost £4 each and can serve up to eight people - ideal for outdoor picnics and garden parties.

    The new additions are available in Tesco stores now and are also available online. But the range will be rolled out to Asda and Morrisons stores over the summer.

    Speaking about the new additions, Jordan McCann, Brand Manager for Finsbury Food Group, said: “We’re incredibly enthusiastic about the launch with TGI Fridays, and already see huge opportunity to continue to add more exciting desserts in the future. After all, we could all do with that Friday’s feeling!”

    Tesco shoppers can also save some money thanks to a limited time offer from TopCashback.

    From now until April 25, new and existing TopCashback members will be able to earn 8% cashback (£15 maximum cashback) with no minimum spend when shopping in store at Tesco.

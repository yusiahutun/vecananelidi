    29 Feb 2024, 00:00

    The stars are coming out in full force for Paris Fashion Week, and of course, Queen Zendaya did not disappoint. She even debuted a new hairstyle which is taking Paris, and the world by storm.

    The celebrity-filled Schiaparelli Spring/Summer 2024 event welcomed an array of A-list faces, but Zendaya stood out in her dramatic black ensemble. So big and bold, that she needed someone’s help walking down the carpet. Big slay.

    Zendaya ‘takes over’ Paris Fashion Week

    Honestly, we don’t know where to start because Zendaya absolutely slayed every aspect. Starting with her hair. The 27-year-old had all eyes on her as she debuted short, choppy bangs with sleek straight hair, resting below her shoulders.

    Once fans got over her face card, it was time for her show-stopping gown. The Hollywood star opted for. dramatic high neck all-black gown with a satin skirt and matching, extremely long, black train. 

    So long, that she needed some help holding it up as she posed for the pictures everyone is now drooling over.

    Finishing off the all-black look, the Euphoria star, who is currently dating Tom Holland, wore sheer black tights with some stylish black pumps.

    “That Zendaya serve woke up something in me,” tweeted one fan.

    Another said: “Need Zendaya cast as a villain NOW.”

    “I’m not surprised at all but the fact that she can pull those bags off with no problem is making me a little sick tbh like leave some for us bae,” joked another.

    “That’s a goddess right there,” fangirled a fourth.

    Euphoria co-stars reunite

    As if her look wasn’t enough, the star also sent fans into a meltdown as she was reunited with Euphoria co-star, Hunter Schafer at the prestigious event.

    The pair played Rue Bennett and Jules Vaughn on the hit HBO TV series, and as fans have been waiting on what feels like forever for the new season, they’ll take what they can get.

    Schafer also stunned in a black gown, which she accessorized with a gold flower piece around her neck.

    Of course, fans went wild on X, formerly known as Twitter at the sight of the two together. “
    ZENDAYA AND HUNTER SCHAFER OMG RUE AND JULES,” screamed one.

    Another wrote: “They just look so cool together.” Honestly, we agree.

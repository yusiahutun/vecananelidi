    Kanye West’s ex-girlfriend Julia Fox, whom he dated last year, has been spotted having quite the fashion disaster.

    The actress was out on the town last night, October 18, at Ham Yard Hotel in London and, like Kanye West’s new ‘wife’ Bianca Censori, wore something that was very out of the box … or should we say dry cleaning?

    Julia Fox’s whacky wardrobe risks fashion disaster

    She was papped wearing a literal bag of dry cleaning. But that’s high-fashion for you.

    Hanging around the movie star’s neck, she had a see-through, plastic bag that contained an oversized, button-down white T-shirt that had the slogan “Figura Services Limited” written on it along with the brand’s logo.

    The 33-year-old looked as though she was about to get hitched as she went all white with her outfit choice. She paired the shirt with a white skirt and knee-high boots, with a white purse to match.

    It’s not the first time a celebrity has been seen in this style though. Both Christine Quinn and Khloé Kardashian have been spotted wearing their fashion ensemble on the outside as part of the design.

    Fans convinced ‘Kanye West has a type’

    After Julia showed off her body in a barely-there bikini, fans took to social media to say that they couldn’t cope with her fashion sense and that her ex-boyfriend likes a certain type of woman.

    One fan wrote: “Unnecessary! She does have great legs though.”

    Another said: “Kanye has a type.”

    A third penned: “Julia Fox’s outfits stress me out every f***** time.”

    It comes after Kanye and his new wife Bianca Censori have been making headlines with their fits. Bianca, who likes to cause a storm with skintight outfits, has been apparently warned by the Italian government to tone it down. The celebrity couple have been enjoying a long vacation overseas in Europe and caused a stir with their antics.

    Julia claims being with Kanye ‘felt like having two babies’

    It comes after Julia has been talking about her ex Kanye and what happened in their short time together. The model has written a memoir, called Down The Drain, and teases Ye is in there.

    The mom-of-one said that while with the rapper, it felt like she had another child and that she thought he needed a “full-time person” which she couldn’t be for him.

    Talking to Drew Barrymore on her talk show on Tuesday (October 18), Julia was candid about their relationship.

    She said: “I only could do it for so long because ultimately I think he needed a full-time person and I just couldn’t be full-time.

    “I had my son [Valentino] and then he’d wanna talk on the phone a lot, like, I’d have to change diapers so I bought a pair of AirPods, so I could keep it in while I was doing mom stuff. It was just — it was so overwhelming and so unsustainable.”

    Julia shares her two-year-old with ex-husband Peter Artemiev, who she divorced in 2020 after two years of marriage.

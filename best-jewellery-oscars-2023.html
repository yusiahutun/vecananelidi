    The 10 best jewellery moments from the 2023 Oscars

    Deepika Padukone wore one of the most breathtaking pieces of jewellery on the night, a diamond necklace by Cartier which featured a giant yellow sapphire. She teamed it with a diamond bracelet and two rings, a perfect accompaniment to her velvet Louis Vuitton gown.

    02 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Deepika Padukone in Cartier

    Kayla Oaddams//Getty Images

    Deepika Padukone wore one of the most breathtaking pieces of jewellery on the night, a diamond necklace by Cartier which featured a giant yellow sapphire. She teamed it with a diamond bracelet and two rings, a perfect accompaniment to her velvet Louis Vuitton gown.

    Cara Delevingne in Bulgari

    Lexie Moreland//Getty Images

    Cara Delevingne was all about Old Hollywood glamour in her one-shouldered Elie Saab gown and her striking jewels. The model wore a Bulgari Serpenti necklace in white gold, adding earrings, a bracelet and a ring from the same collection.

    Rihanna in Moussaieff

    Jeff Kravitz//Getty Images

    For her red-carpet appearance, Rihanna accessorised her Alaïa gown with a pair of brown, green and yellow diamond earrings, complemented by matching rings. When she changed for her on-stage performance, she was dripping in De Beers diamonds.

    Emily Blunt in Chopard

    Arturo Holmes//Getty Images

    Emily Blunt accessorised her sleek and simple off-the-shoulder Valentino gown with a pair of statement purple earrings by Chopard.

    Jessica Chastain in Gucci

    Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

    Actress Jessica Chastain was all about the sparkles on Sunday night, teaming her sequin dress with some glitzy jewels, both courtesy of Gucci. The statement choker was an 18-carat white-gold necklace featuring tsavorites and diamonds.

    Cate Blanchett in Louis Vuitton

    Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

    Cate Blanchett accessorised her Louis Vuitton dress with a pair of white gold and diamond double droplet earrings from the house's high jewellery collection. Simple and elegant.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Halle Bailey in De Beers

    Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

    Halle Bailey added some sparkle to her Dolce & Gabbana ensemble with a little help from De Beers. The actress wore a diamond necklace set in white gold with diamond and platinum stud earrings, as well as two statement rings.

    Margot Robbie in Lorraine Schwartz

    Rich Polk//Getty Images

    She may have skipped the red carpet, but Margot Robbie did not hold back when it came to her Oscars look. The actress accessorised her Armani Privé gown with 72-carat emerald, black jade and diamond earrings by Lorraine Schwartz, with a matching ring.

    Florence Pugh in Tiffany & Co

    Mike Coppola//Getty Images

    Florence Pugh wore a dramatic platinum diamond necklace by Tiffany & Co, as well as earrings with pink tourmalines and diamonds by the brand, to glitz up her Valentino ensemble.

    Elizabeth Banks in Pomellato

    ABC//Getty Images

    Elizabeth Banks chose a rose-gold diamond set from Pomellato – which featured a choker necklace, hoop earrings and a ring – to accessorise her Vivienne Westwood gown.

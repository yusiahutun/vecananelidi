Her father’s success as the Dallas Mavericks point guard has bequeathed him enormous fame, which has rubbed off on those around him.

    Consequently, many fans are eager to learn about Azurie’s early life, age, siblings, and other details. Likewise, there is curiosity surrounding her mother and the nature of her parents’ relationship. Fans want to know if they are still together.

    Here’s everything we know about Kyrie Irving’s First child. 

    How Old Is Azurie Elizabeth Irving? 

    Azurie Elizabeth Irving, born on November 23, 2015, in Dallas, Texas, USA, is the daughter of Kyrie Irving and Andrea Wilson. Hence, she is seven years old and is of African-American descent. In addition, her paternal grandparents are Drederick Irving and Elizabeth Irving, while her maternal grandparents are Arthur Franklin and Verna Wilson.

    What Is the Reason Behind Kyrie Irving’s Daughter’s Name?

    The significance behind Azurie’s name holds a heartfelt meaning. After enduring a 35-hour labor, her father, Kyrie Irving, a then-point guard for the Brooklyn Nets, took to Instagram two days after her birth to express his deep affection for his daughter. In his post, the NBA star shared that he named her after his late mother, Elizabeth, who unfortunately passed away when he was four. 

    Kyrie’s Instagram caption conveyed his love for Azurie, stating that she brings a radiant joy to his life that he never imagined possible. In addition, he expressed his belief that by giving her his mother’s name, the same blessings his mother bestowed upon him and their family would also be upon Azurie. The post concluded with a heartfelt declaration of love for his mother.

    Who Is Azurie Elizabeth Irving’s Father?

    Azurie Elizabeth Irving’s father, Kyrie Andrew Irving, is a well-known and accomplished basketball player. He began his NBA career with the Cleveland Cavaliers, where he played from 2011 to 2017.

    Kyrie received the prestigious NBA Rookie of the Year Award while he was with the team. In addition, he earned his first selection to the NBA All-Star Game, contributing 15 points, four assists, and three rebounds in 2013.

    One of Kyrie’s most memorable moments occurred in 2016 when he made a game-winning three-pointer against the Golden State Warriors. However, he later requested a trade and joined the Boston Celtics, where he played for two consecutive years before eventually moving to the Brooklyn Nets.

    In February 2020, Kyrie became the vice president of the National Basketball Players Association, demonstrating his involvement and leadership within the basketball community. However, after playing with the Brooklyn Nets from 2019-2023, Kyrie moved to the Dallas Mavericks, where he currently plays.

    Aside from his successful basketball career, Azurie’s father has ventured into entertainment. He has appeared in movies such as “Kickin’ It” (2012) and has done voicework for animated shows like “We Bare Bears” (2016) and “Family Guy” (2018). 

    Who Is Kyrie Irving’s Daughter’s Mother?

    Andrea Wilson is the mother of Azurie Elizabeth Irving. She is a popular model born on February 8, 1986, in Dallas, Texas, USA. She is currently 37 years old and falls under the astrological sign of Aquarius. In addition, Andrea has two siblings named Lindsay and Anthony. 

    Azurie’s mum isn’t very public; details about her educational background and the institutions she attended remain unknown. However, Andrea gained popularity by winning the “Miss Texas” title in 2010 and being the ex-girlfriend of Kyrie Irving. Aside from these, there is limited information about her career and professional life. 

    Are Azurie Elizabeth Irving’s Parents Together?

    No, they are not. Kyrie Irving and Andrea Wilson’s love story began in 2012 when they first met, but they didn’t start dating until July 2013. Their relationship was joyful at first. Soon after the duo started dating, Andrea became pregnant with their daughter, Azurie Elizabeth Irving.

    Unfortunately, their relationship encountered difficulties, leading them to part ways. The couple officially separated in April 2015, seven months before Azurie’s birth. However, Andrea spoke out in August 2015, accusing Kyrie of leaving her unsupported during her pregnancy. The former beauty queen claimed to have shouldered all expenses and responsibilities without his assistance, emphasizing her lack of connection with him.

    Although Kyrie denied these allegations, labeling them false, he filed legal documents requesting a paternity test to confirm his fatherhood. Afterward, he expressed his desire to fully support their child, emphasizing his intention to be an involved father. In addition, he stated that he would cover all expenses for the child but requested that Andrea raise their child in Texas.

    Despite the initial conflicts, Azurie’s parents have managed to resolve things, maintain an amicable relationship, and co-parent their child. According to reports, the NBA star pays $4500 monthly in child support.

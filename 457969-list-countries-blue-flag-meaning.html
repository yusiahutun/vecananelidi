    List of all countries with blue on their flag and its meaning

    A national flag is a country's identity. Flags are designed to preserve the countries' histories and cultures. They have different colours and designs, which are interpreted differently. So, which are the countries with blue on their flags and what does it mean?

    A national flag is a country's identity. Flags are designed to preserve the countries' histories and cultures. They have different colours and designs, which are interpreted differently. So, which are the countries with blue on their flags and what does it mean?

    There are many countries with blue on their flag. The blue colour is blended with different colours and symbols. The colour may represent history, culture, core values, and natural resources.

    Countries with blue on their flag

    Every country has a flag with distinctive designs and colours. The design and colour represent different meanings according to each country. Here are the countries with blue flags of the world with their meaning.

    1. Argentina

    Argentina is a country in the southern half of South America, and it is a country with blue flags. The country's flag is a triband, composed of three equally wide horizontal bands coloured light blue and white. The blue and white symbolise the clear skies and snow of the Andes.

    2. El Salvador

    El Salvador is located in Central America. It is bordered on the northeast by Honduras, on the northwest by Guatemala, and on the south by the Pacific Ocean. Its capital city is San Salvador.

    The country's flag features a horizontal triband of cobalt blue-white-cobalt blue. It has the coat of arms at the centre and is entirely contained within the central white stripe. The blue represents the two oceans surrounding Central America and the great sky.

    3. Finland

    Finland is a Nordic country in Northern Europe. The country borders Sweden, Norway, and Russia. Its flag features a blue Nordic cross on a white background. The blue Nordic cross represents Christianity.

    4. Greece

    Greece is a country in Southeast Europe. The county's flag features nine horizontal stripes of white alternating with blue. It also has a blue canton on the upper hoist-side with a cross.

    The flag's nine stripes represent the nine letters in the term freedom. The blue colour symbolises the colours of the Greek sea or sky.

    5. Guatemala

    Guatemala is in Central America, located in the south of Mexico. It is home to volcanoes, rainforests and ancient Mayan sites. Its flag features two colours: Sky blue and white.

    Sky blue stripes represent the fact that Guatemala is a land located between two oceans. These land masses are the Pacific Ocean and the Atlantic Ocean. The colour also represents the sky over the country.

    6. Honduras

    Honduras is a Central American country. It has the Caribbean Sea coastlines to the north and the Pacific Ocean to the south. The country's flag has horizontal blue-white-blue stripes with five central blue stars.

    The two blue bands represent the Pacific Ocean and the Caribbean Sea, the sky and brotherhood. White represents peace, the prosperity of Hondurans, and purity of thoughts.

    7. Israel

    Israel is a Middle Eastern country on the Mediterranean Sea. Jews, Christians, and Muslims regard it as the Biblical Holy Land, and its most sacred sites are in Jerusalem. Its flag features a blue hexagram on a white background, between two horizontal blue stripes.

    The blue stripes are intended to represent the stripes on a tallit, the traditional Jewish prayer shawl. It symbolises God's glory, purity, and God's severity.

    8. Micronesia

    Micronesia is a subregion of Oceania. It consists of about two thousand small islands in the western Pacific Ocean. Its flag has a light blue field with four white five-pointed stars arranged in the diamond pattern in the centre. The light blue field represents the Pacific Ocean.

    9. Nicaragua

    Nicaragua is a Central American nation. It is set between the Pacific Ocean and the Caribbean Sea. It is also known for its dramatic terrain of lakes, volcanoes, and beaches.

    Nicaragua has a horizontally striped blue-white-blue national flag with a central coat of arms. Traditionally, the blue colour represented the Pacific Ocean and the Caribbean Sea. Today, the colour blue symbolises justice and loyalty.

    10. Somalia

    Somalia is a country in the Horn of Africa. Its national flag consists of a light blue field with a central white star. The blue symbolises the surrounding Indian Ocean and the blue sky above.

    11. The Czech Republic

    The Czech Republic is located in Central Europe. It is a landlocked country. Its national flag has two horizontal stripes of white over red with a blue triangle at the hoist. The blue colour in the flag reprints the State of Moravia.

    12. Sweden

    Sweden's flag consists of a yellow or gold Nordic Cross on a field of light blue. The blue colour represents perseverance, vigilance, truth, loyalty, and justice. The gold and yellow colours represent the generosity of the Swedish.

    13. Russia

    Russia is a transcontinental country spanning Eastern Europe and Northern Asia. It is the largest country in the world by area. The country has a tricolour flag.

    It consists of three equal horizontal fields: white on the top, blue in the middle, and red on the bottom. The blue colour represents faithfulness, honesty, impeccability, and chastity.

    14. Haiti

    Haiti is a Caribbean country. The country shares the island of Hispaniola with the Dominican Republic to its east. It was the world's first black-led republic and the first independent Caribbean state.

    Haiti's flag has a horizontal bicolour of blue and red. It is charged with the Coat of Arms in a small white box in the centre. The blue represented Haiti's African residents, while the red represented those of mixed European and African descent.

    15. The Central African Republic

    The Central African Republic is a landlocked country, and it is located in Central Africa. The country's flag has four horizontal stripes of blue, white, green, and yellow intersected by one vertical strip of red in the middle. The blue colour stands for liberty, grandeur, and the sky.

    16. South Korea

    South Korea is an East Asian nation on the southern half of the Korean Peninsula. The flag colours are red, blue, and black on a white background. The colour represents peace and purity.

    17. Venezuela

    Venezuela is a country on the northern coast of South America. The country's flag has a horizontally striped yellow-blue-red national flag with an arc of eight white stars in the centre. The blue colour represents courage.

    18. Brazil

    Brazil is one of the largest countries in the world. It is a country in both South America and Latin America. The country's flag has a blue disc spanned by a curved band.

    The band is inscribed with the national motto "Ordem e Progresso" within a yellow rhombus on a green field. The blue globe represents the night sky.

    19. South Africa

    South Africa is the southernmost country in Africa. It is one of the many countries with flags containing blue. The national flag consists of a horizontally oriented Y-shape in green, with yellow and white borders. Separating the sections of red and blue and a black hoist triangle.

    20. Ukraine

    Ukraine's flag consists of equally sized horizontal bands of blue and yellow. It was officially adopted in 1992 when the Soviet Empire disbanded. The blue colour denotes the skies over the vast land.

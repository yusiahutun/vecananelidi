    Megan Barton-Hanson has claimed she was forced to delete OnlyFans before her Love Island stint.

    Speaking on the Smut Drop podcast, the 28-year-old model recounted the pressure she felt from a producer of the ITV series to drop her affiliation with the site, which she had before entering the villa.

    Megan said that she was encouraged to abandon her OnlyFans, which she also confirmed as her "main source of income", and followed that advice as she was told it "wouldn't be good for her or the show's image".

    On the podcast, Megan said: "Before I went on Love Island, I had OnlyFans and it was the production team who said, 'Look, this is a family show.

    This easy cocktail is like a set of icicle lights in a glass: It's classy, sparkly, and oh-so-Christmasy.  

    Read More

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Yields: 1 serving(s)

    Prep Time: 5 mins

    Total Time: 5 mins

    Ingredients

    Save Recipe
    • 1 oz.

      peppermint schnapps, plus more for glass rim

    • Finely crushed starlight mints, for rim

    • Prosecco or other sparkling wine

    Directions

    • Step 1Place peppermint schnapps on a small plate. Place mints on a second small plate. Dip the rim of a Champagne glass in the schnapps then in the mints. Pour schnapps into prepared glass and top with Prosecco.

    It seems we've followed along on Poppy Delevingne's wedding adventure from the beginning—starting with an Instagram image of her engagement ring and the creative approach fiance James Cook took to asking the question. Today, the model is saying "I do" at St Paul's Church in Knightsbridge and images have already been released of her in a stunning Chanel Haute Couture gown by Karl Lagerfeld. With floral appliques, a cap sleeve and drop waist, the gown is absolutely stunning with a subtle bohemian vibe. Delevingne wears her hair down with a slight wave and long veil—her makeup has been done by Charlotte Tilbury and her hair is in the capable hands of Mr George Northwood, according to Grazia UK.

    Poppy is joined at her side by sister and maid of honor Cara Delevingne, also in white Chanel. Congratulations to the lovely couple.

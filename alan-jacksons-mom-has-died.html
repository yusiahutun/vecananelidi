    Alan Jackson Mom Died - Ruth Jackson Died

    Though 2017 has just begun, we've already been hit with some sad news: Beloved country star Alan Jackson's mother passed away on Saturday morning. Jackson shared the sad news with his fans on Instagram.

    02 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Though 2017 has just begun, we've already been hit with some sad news: Beloved country star Alan Jackson's mother passed away on Saturday morning. Jackson shared the sad news with his fans on Instagram.

    The post reads:

    It is with heavy hearts that we share the passing of Alan's beloved mother - Ruth Jackson: January 7, 2017 – Mrs. Ruth Jackson – known to her family, friends and country music fans the world over simply as "Mama Ruth" – died Saturday morning, January 7, peacefully at her home in Newnan, Georgia. Mrs. Jackson became a celebrated part of son Alan Jackson's career and life story as the inspiration behind some of his fans' favorite music. Mama Ruth was 86.

    Ruth has been the inspiration behind much of Jackson's music, including his album of gospel hymns, Precious Memories, and his Let It Be Christmas album, which Jackson recorded after Ruth said she wanted to hear her son sing holiday classics. Jackson's song "Home" also details his parents' early married years.

    In the wake of her death, an old video of Ruth singing along to one of Jackson's performances has resurfaced, with fans sharing it on social media as a tribute to Ruth and the love she had for her son.

    Our thoughts and prayers are with Jackson and his family during this difficult time.

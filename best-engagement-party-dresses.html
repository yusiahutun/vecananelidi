    Dumebi Andrea Iyamah, of her eponymous label Andrea Iyamah, is a Nigerian-Canadian fashion designer born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria. With her flair for complimentary silhouettes, tropical prints, and elevated details, she has solidified her brand as the embodiment of a much-needed getaway—engagement dresses included.

    A Cold-Shouldered Mini

    Rotate Ruched Twill Mini Dress

    A Cold-Shouldered Mini

    Rotate Ruched Twill Mini Dress

    It-girl duo Thora Valdimars and Jeanette Madsen founded Rotate, a Scandi-favorite brand, out of pure joy for impish clothing. Embracing the label’s penchant for ’80s silhouettes, this mini will ensure your engagement party photos are ones to look back on fondly. Pair it with a strappy nude heel and let your hair down for the night.

    Sizes: 0–12

    A Sequined Showstopper

    Christopher John Rogers Open-Back Sequin-Embellished Midi Dress

    A Sequined Showstopper

    Christopher John Rogers Open-Back Sequin-Embellished Midi Dress

    There are people who casually enjoy clothing, and then there are Christopher John Rogers fans—a vast fan base of vibrant “squirrels” who find solace in expressing themselves through carefully constructed garments. For a pop of color, team this embellished midi with a fuchsia pump or satin-heeled sandal.

    Sizes: XS–XL

    A Summer Staple

    The Garment Jagger Long Dress

    A Summer Staple

    The Garment Jagger Long Dress

    Now 34% Off

    The Garment was founded in Copenhagen by Sophia Roe and Charlotte Eskildsen, so it’s no surprise each piece from the brand is consciously constructed and minimalist. For example: the Jagger dress, an organic cotton sleeveless maxi with a ruched bodice and tie details for an engagement party held in a garden.

    Sizes: 6–12

    A Puff-Sleeved Mini

    Solace London Hayes One-Shoulder Puff-Sleeve Mini Dress

    A Puff-Sleeved Mini

    Solace London Hayes One-Shoulder Puff-Sleeve Mini Dress

    Solace London seeks out the spotlight, and that’s evident in every piece the brand releases. Just take a look at the Hayes dress. The asymmetrical profile is punctuated by a single puffed sleeve that offers surprising volume. This mini dress pairs best with barely-there heeled sandals.

    Sizes: XXS–L

    A Satin Dress With a Bell Sleeve

    Reformation Maves Satin Dress

    A Satin Dress With a Bell Sleeve

    Reformation Maves Satin Dress

    If the dress code calls for dancing, this sophisticated silk mini checks all the party-ready boxes. The fluid neckline is surprisingly structured, offering freedom in the form of a bra-free evening. It pairs well with a ’60s mod Mary Jane or platform.

    Sizes: XS–XL

    A Sexy, Sleeveless Option

    ELOQUII Twisted Top Dress

    A Sexy, Sleeveless Option

    ELOQUII Twisted Top Dress

    Now 49% Off

    For a beachside soiree or a warm-weather bash, ELOQUII has created a midi dress that epitomizes summer pleasure. Flattering in all the right ways, it features a shape-hugging stretch-knit fabric, an open wrap skirt that ties at the front, and a simmering cutout at the waist.

    Sizes: 14–28.

    A Boudoir-Style Dress

    Nensi Dojaka Lace-Trimmed Mini Dress

    A Boudoir-Style Dress

    Nensi Dojaka Lace-Trimmed Mini Dress

    Nensi Dojaka reinterprets lingerie codes with a modern flair, as seen in this silky mini dress—unapologetically sexy and brimming with fantasy. Featuring slip-inspired styling, this lined style has ruched cups, an asymmetrical hemline, and lace trimmings for the bride who dares to dream.

    Sizes: XXS–L

    A Crochet Maxi

    Cult Gaia Everly Crochet Maxi Dress

    A Crochet Maxi

    Cult Gaia Everly Crochet Maxi Dress

    Now 41% Off

    You can never go wrong with a little white dress, and this one offers all the detail without the added complications. The Everly dress from Cult Gaia is made from crocheted cotton, with a cutout bodice, crossover straps, and a scalloped hem—ideal for engagement parties held beside a rooftop pool.

    Sizes: XS–XL

    A Dress With a Tulle Accent

    Maison Margiela Tulle-Wrapped Midi Dress

    A Dress With a Tulle Accent

    Maison Margiela Tulle-Wrapped Midi Dress

    Martin Margiela shaped ’90s fashion with his deconstructed silhouettes and artistic expression, showcased in this luxe midi dress. This Italian-made dress boasts a simple cotton weave punctuated by frothy black tulle encircling the waist. A true fashion bride!

    Sizes: 2–8

    A Column Midi Dress

    Mara Hoffman Maia Floral-Appliquéd Cotton-Linen Midi Dress

    A column silhouette and eco-friendly fabrics give a nod to Mara Hoffman’s conscious ethos. A fresh spin on a classic strapless dress, the Maia dress is made from Tencel and linen, and defined by a large floral appliqué that will surely turn heads at your engagement party.

    Sizes: 0–10

    Tove Juliet Ivory Dress

    A Timeless, Strapless Maxi Dress

    Tove Juliet Ivory Dress

    Now 22% Off

    Cotton can absolutely be worn for bridal occasions, no matter how lavish. Just look to Tove’s signature details: draping, lush gathering, and seamless constructions. All of the above are included in the Juliet dress, a piece so transitional it can be worn during a party or to the farmers’ market.

    Bernadette Tilly Off-the-Shoulder Taffeta Mini Dress

Crisp taffeta and an off-the-shoulder neckline allow for dangling jewels, while the short hemline pairs perfectly with sky-high heels.

    A Printed Midi Dress

    A Printed Midi Dress

    Yuhan Wang Bustier-Seam Floral Print Midi Dress

    Now 50% Off

    Don’t be tricked by the sweetness displayed on the front of this dress—there’s a party out back, and it’s sultry. It’s made with a corseted bodice that provides structure and support, while the skirt remains free and flowing.

    A Sophisticated Mini

    Khaite Hila Pleated Halterneck Dress

    A Sophisticated Mini

    Khaite Hila Pleated Halterneck Dress

    Credit: Net-a-Porter

    What comes to mind when you think of Khaite, besides the luxe furry pants that debuted at the brand’s latest runway show? If I could give Khaite three adjectives, they’d be: polished, timeless, and just a bit dainty. Part of the label’s Resort ’23 collection, the Hila dress is made from crisp white twill, with a halter neck and pleated skirt that says “married woman.”

    Markarian The Date Sequined Slip Dress

The NYC-based luxury womenswear label prides itself on being both striking and modern, notes you’ll see in the Date dress.

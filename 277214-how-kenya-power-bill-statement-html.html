    With the recent upgrade of the Kenya Power Customer Management System, electricity consumers all over the country have a reason to smile. This system comes with lots of benefits including prompt electricity bill updates, improved customer care response time and self-services modules to customers. However, before we make merry about this benefits we need to ask ourselves, how can one check their Kenya power bill statement easily? In case you have been having similar concerns, this article is for you. At the end of this read, you should be able to check your Kenya power bill statement from different devices at your disposal at the comfort of your home. Let’s get to it.

    Electricity users in Kenya are the biggest gainers of the recent advancements in technology. Forget the old days where all you had was to wait for the monthly Kenya Power bill statement at your post box and say hello to the digital age. Now you don’t have to wait for your electricity bill to accumulate before you make the payments. Thanks to the comfort of the Kenya Power Customer Management System (CMS) you can constantly check your electricity consumption and make payments on a regular basis. How, you may ask? Below is a step by step process on how to check Kenya power bill statement.

    How to check Kenya power statement via SMS

    Mobile phones are on the rise in Kenya. Thanks to Kenya Power it’s time these device were put into good use; other than socializing and communicating. Through the SMS service Kenyans from all divides can check and receive their electricity bill with less. With only Ksh. 5 that will be deducted as transaction costs, you should be able to know your kenya power statement. Below are the simple prompts you should follow.

    • Go to the messaging menu on your mobile device
    • Key in the first six digits of your power meter number on the message field, for instance for a 557799-01 account type in 557799
    • Send the message to 95551
    • After a few minutes, an SMS reply from KPLC will be sent to your mobile phone with details of your account balance, account number and name

    How to Check Kenya power bill statement via email

    The second method to check power bill statement is through email. Yes, for tech savvy people this is one of the most sort after method. It’s simple to check your bill statement using this option and the best part of it is that it doesn’t cost you a shilling. As long as you have a steady internet connection you are as good as done. For those wondering how to get kenya power bill statement using this process, the following prompts should guide you.

    • First, open your browse on whatever device you are using, it could be your smatphone, you desktop or even laptop
    • Open your email account and create a new mail in it
    • On the subject field enter the first part of your Kenya power account, that is 557799 for 557799-01.
    • Add bill@kplc.co.ke the email section and send a black mail. Wait for a few minute and check your inbox for KPLC’s reply.

    Check KPLC bill statement via portal

    If you want get your kenya power online bill statement the other option you can use is their portal. Of the three options, this is the most convenient and effective option. Why? With the Kenya power portal, once you have keyed your meter number, your will be able to get your bill statement there and then. Did I mention that you don’t have to pay for anything to access this service. Check your Kenya power bill statement online even this moment by visiting the official KPLC site.

    Note, even with the above three options in place you will still be able to receive your monthly Kenya power bill statement via your mailbox. It is important to ensure you keep track of these receipts and ensure that whatever you are paying for reflects on the bill posted.

    Kenya power bill payment

    Now that we have helped you answer the trivial question, how can i get my kenya power bill statement, the next important thing we need to do is help you pay the bill. Late payment or default accumulates dire consequences such as disconnection. Just like checking your bill statement, bill payment can also be done online. There are many ways to pay your electricity consumption bill with the two most common options being through Mpesa and Airtel money. The following is how to go about it.

    How to Pay KPLC electricity bill via Mpesa

    This option is only applicable to Safaricom subscribers.

  • Go to your Safaricom Sim Kit on your device
  • Choose Mpesa
  • Then select the Pay Bill option from the menu
  • Enter 888 888 Kenya Power’s business number
  • Key in your full Kenya Power meter number, that is 113355-01.
  • Input any amount you’d wish to pay, however it should always range between Ksh. 100 - Ksh.35,000.
  • Enter your secure M-Pesa PIN
  • Confirm the details
  • Lastly a confirmation message will be sent via SMS confirming payment received.

    How to pay your electricity bill with Airtel Money

    For Airtel users the payment process is pretty much similar to that of Mpesa; however, there are certain things to take note of. The following is how you pay your electricity bill Via Airtel money.

  • Choose Airtel Money from your Sim kit menu
  • Go to “Make Payments”
  • Choose the paybill option
  • For postpaid bills select appropriately for either prepaid or postpaid bills
  • Choose the sum amount you’d wish to purchase
  • Then enter secure Airtel Money PIN
  • On the reference section, enter your full Power meter number, for example 224466-01.
  • Verify all your details as correct and proceed
  • An SMS confirming the success of the process will be sent directly to your phone.

    • Avoid power interruptions by checking your Kenya power bill statement early enough and equally making your payments in time. Note that for both Airtel and Mpesa payment options this process takes upto 48 hours for the money to reflect on your account therefore it is important to make your payments early enough. However in case inquires you can always reach out to the kenya power customer care assistants who will be glad to help you resolve your issue faster.

    They may be primmer than a lot of A-list actresses but Keira Knightley and Nicole Kidman are like you’ve never seen them before in a new photo shoot.

    The two stars strip off to pose for some racy pictures that appear in the latest issue of Interview magazine.

    Keira goes topless for her artistic black and white shoot with Patrick Demarchelier, in which she sports nothing but lace opera gloves and black trousers, along with wet hair.

    Scroll down for video

    Artistic: Keira Knightley's wet hair is plastered against her face in some of black and white shots by Patrick Demarchelier

    Keira bares all: Ms. Knightley goes topless for the September issue of Interview magazine


      

    When the 29-year-old wasn’t half-naked, she was instead lounging around in stunning black evening gowns.

    Meanwhile, Nicole can be seen posing in sexy cream satin lingerie, complete with stockings and suspenders.

    The 47-year-old looks incredible in the purple lit photos as she lies on the dirty ground in the expensive underwear and a pair of high heels, with a man on top of her in one.

    Stripping off: Nicole Kidman also stars in a shoot for the magazine, while clad in cream satin lingerie

    Princess dress: When she wasn't half-naked, Keira was clad in black evening gowns

    Naomi Campbell also took part in a shoot for the magazine, going topless on a tennis court while clad in black knickers, gold heels and with a leopard print coat over her shoulders.

    The supermodel then poses beside a swimming pool with a car sinking in it, in a long black dress with a thigh-high split up one side.

    As well as their separate photo shoots, the stars appear on alternative covers of the magazine.

    While Keira was shot by Patrick, Nicole was captured by Stephen Klein and Naomi by Mert Alas and Marcus Piggott.

     

    Up close and personal: A man can be seen climbing on top of Nicole while she plays the damsel

    Film noir style: The 47-year-old can also be seen in suspenders, stockings and heels as she lies on the dirty ground

    Serving up some sex appeal: Naomi Campbell goes topless for the magazine too, and poses on a tennis court

    Keira’s cover depicts her with wet hair while clad in one of the frocks, while in Nicole’s film noir style image the man, who has a scratched face, appears to bite her neck.

    Interview's September issue has been dubbed 'The Photographer's Issue', and the magazine asked six photographers to capture on film their 'ideal woman.'

    The photographers chose a fair mix of film stars and supermodels that also included Lea Sydoux, Amber Valletta and Daria Werbowy.

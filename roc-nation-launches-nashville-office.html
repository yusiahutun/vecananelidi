    Roc Nation Launches Nashville Office

    26 Feb 2024, 00:00

    NASHVILLE (CelebrityAccess) — Jay Z's music and entertainment company Roc Nation, has launched a new office in Nashville.

    At least initially, Roc Nation's new office will be focused around songwriter/producer/publisher/DJ Jesse Frasure and his new venture Rhythm House, which will oversee Frasier's songwriting and production as well as the signing and development of other composers and producers.

    Frasure, who was most recently Vice President of A&R for Major Bob Music, has produced, co-written, or remixed releases by Thomas Rhett, Florida Georgia Line, Meghan Trainor, Rascal Flatts, Who Is Fancy, Luke Bryan, Gary Allan, Billy Currington, Hot Chelle Rae, Chris Lane, The Cadillac Three, and others.

    Frasure recently scored three No. 1 songs as a songwriter on Florida Georgia Line’s “Sun Daze,” Thomas Rhett’s “Crash & Burn,” and Rascal Flatts’ “I Like The Sound of That.” He also co-produced Rhett’s sophomore album, Tangled Up, with producer Dann Huff. As well, he performs as a DJ under the pseudonym Telemitry

    Roc Nation and Rhythm House will work with Warner/Chappell Music for publishing.

    “There is nothing like working with the right individuals, at the right time, forming the right partnership," said Jay Brown, Roc Nation co-founder, and president. "Launching Roc Nation Nashville with Warner/Chappell Music Nashville is a natural extension of the relationship we started so many years ago, and our venture with Jesse Frasure and his Rhythm House take the possibilities to a next level." – Staff Writers

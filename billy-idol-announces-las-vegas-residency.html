    Billy Idol Announces Las Vegas Residency

    30 Apr 2024, 00:00

    LAS VEGAS (CelebrityAccess) –Bill Idol has today (Sept. 18) announced a Las Vegas residency show at the Palms Casino Resort.

    Billy Idol: Las Vegas 2019 presented by SiriusXM, begins January 18, 2019, and continues with dates throughout January and October.

    The show is set to feature material from throughout Idol’s career, including from his days fronting Generation X through to his wide array of solo hits including “Rebel Yell,” “White Wedding,” “Dancing With Myself,” and countless others.

    Idol will be joined by his longtime guitarist Steve Stevens and the rest of the Billy Idol band.

    Commenting on the news, Palms Casino Resort General Manager Jon Gray, said: “Billy Idol is an absolute icon in the music world – one of those artists that people of all ages around the world know the lyrics to every song. It’s an honor to have him back at The Pearl at the Palms. The Billy Idol: Las Vegas 2019 residency perfectly complements the diverse range of musical acts we’re booking at the Pearl and we know these shows will resonate with our audience.”

    Added Idol, “Steve Stevens, my band and I have played Vegas many times and its always been a blast. We can’t stay away! Las Vegas keeps us coming back for more. Let’s make these shows at the Pearl Theater at Palms Casino Resort the best yet!”

    Idol’s latest album, Vital Idol: Revitalized, is due out via Capitol/UMe on September 28th.

    Billy Idol: Las Vegas 2019 Dates:

    • Jan. 18-19, 23, 25-26
    • Oct. 4-5, 9, 11-12

    Jim Chapman and his wife Sarah Tarleton have announced that they have welcomed their second child together.

    The former YouTube star, 35, and his wife took to Instagram on Wednesday to share a joint post confirming their jovial baby news.

    They posted an adorable black-and-white photograph of their newborn son sleeping as they announced the birth of their second child.

    Alongside the snap, they also confirmed their son's name as they wrote: 'He's here… Jesse Patrick Chapman.'

    Jim's fellow YouTube stars were quick to flock to the comments section to share their congratulations with the happy couple.

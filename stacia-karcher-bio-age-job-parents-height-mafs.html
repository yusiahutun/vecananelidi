    Hit TV show, Married at First Sight, is back for its 15th season and this time it is headed to the sunny shores of San Diego. Like previous seasons of MAFS, the couples who were matched by the show’s matchmaking experts will tie the wedding knot first, then head out on a honeymoon, before moving in together and making a final decision between a happily ever after and divorce. Nate Barnes and Stacia Karcher are one of the 5 hopeful couples appearing this season.

    Read this article to find out if they are still together. Also, learn about Stacia’s age, job, parents, and other info.

    Married At First Sight (MAFS): Are Stacia Karcher And Nate Barnes Still Together?

    Married at First Sight returned for its 15th season to Lifetime on July 6th, 2022 with a special three-hour season premiere. 10 singles are joined by relationship experts, who will work with them 24/7 throughout the course of 8 weeks to give them their happily ever after.

    Raised by a single father in Las Vegas, Nate always wanted to settle down and have a family with his perfect match. The 34-years-old, who works as a day trader, signed up for MAFS because he wanted “a best friend, partner, and somebody to explore the whole world with.” “I was feeling lonely and definitely wanted to find a perfect match to do life with,” Nate shared with In Touch Weekly ahead of the show’s premiere.

    Attraction will play a major factor in how the eight-week experiment plays out, the day trader shared. Reportedly, he’s looking for “the equal” and explains the experience will “be a drag” if he wasn’t attracted to his wife.

    On the other hand, Stacia was born to a young single mother. Stacia’s grandmother played a major part in her upbringing.

    In an exclusive interview with In Touch Weekly, Stacia shared the most important thing she was looking for in her match was trust. “I want to know wholeheartedly that I can trust my partner with anything. He is a safe place for me to land, to be vulnerable, and that I can trust his intentions,” the accountant told.

    She revealed that she was tired of meeting men that did not meet her high standards.

    Stacia has been ready for marriage for quite some time and is done with the whole dating thing. She also shared a story from one of her dates that ultimately made her refuse to settle. She said, “While on a date, I was told to Cash App him my portion of money for the date. From there I was done.”

    The MAFS star also shared that she did not have any fears going into the show. “If it didn’t work out between me and my husband then that’s OK, we go our separate ways. I go back to living a dope life single.” Stacia told.

    Despite knowing what to expect from the marriage experiment, Stacia shared feeling nervous going to the actual wedding day. “I have the sweats… I wanna throw up, pass out…just wanna get this over with and meet my husband,” Stacia recalled her wedding day during a chat with Entertainment Tonight.

    While both Stacia and Nate fancy different qualities (or traits) from their perfect one, the hopeful couples are on the same page when it comes to their strong desire for family. Coming from single-parent households, both want a partner to create unforgettable experiences with. “Being able to create memories. Being able to have a partner that I could just do this crazy experiment with that was as crazy as I was,” Stacia told In Touch Weekly.

    As of this writing, we did not know if the two are still together. We guess the fans will have to tune into Married at First Sight and wait till Decision Day to know if they opt for a happily ever after or a divorce.

    Stacia Karcher Age

    Probably born in the year 1985, Stacia’s age was 37 at the time of filming the show.

    Stacia Karcher Parents

    As we’ve already told, Stacia was born to her single mother, Stacy Karcher. Since Stacia’s mother was too young to raise a kid, the MAFS star grew up around her grandmother.

    Stacia is close to her mother, Stacy and reportedly she has even made appearances on the hit Lifetime show.

    Is Stacia Karcher On Instagram?

    Stacia Karcher is on Instagram at @stac1a_d, where she has more than 2 thousand followers on the platform. Her IG account, however, was set to private as of July 2022. You can also find her on TikTok and YouTube.

    Stacia Karcher Job

    As we already know, the MAFS star is an accountant by profession. When she is not doing her professional jobs, she becomes a lifestyle blogger and a YouTuber. Her YouTube account, Stacia Karcher, had 5.19 thousand subscribers in July 2022 and most of the videos on her channel were DIYs, challenges, and makeup videos.

    Though Stacia now earns more than $60 thousand a year being an accountant, she shared in one of her videos that there was a time when she did not almost pass her accounting courses.

    Stacia wanted to be a pro soccer player growing up.

    Her net worth was estimated to be under $600 thousand as of 2022.

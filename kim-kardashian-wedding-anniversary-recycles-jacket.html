    04 Jul 2024, 00:00

      With her anniversary fast approaching, Kim Kardashian has clearly had her wedding on her mind. On Thursday, the 34-year-old reality TV star recycled the personalised leather jacket she wore just after tying the knot with Kanye West when she took their daughter North West to her ballet and tap classes in Los Angeles.The black jacket, which bears the word 'married' on the back above an Aztec-inspired image of an eagle, made its debut on 24 May 2014 in a photobooth snapshot of Kim and Kanye – with the rapper wearing a matching jacket reading 'just'.

      Just like her mum, one-year-old North had a mini leather jacket to wear over her black leotard, tutu and tights. The youngster, who proudly sported her heeled tap shoes, wore her hair in two little buns on the top of her head.

      Sunday will mark one year since Kim and Kanye married in a spectacular Italian wedding ceremony.

      Joined by their nearest and dearest at the Forte di Belvedere in Florence, the couple finally said "I do" at 6pm local time.

      Bruce Jenner walked Kim down the aisle as Italian tenor Andrea Bocelli sang several songs, including Speaking to , mother-of-the-bride Kris expressed her joy at seeing her daughter marry: "I'm beyond bursting with happiness for Kim, Kanye and baby North. Such a magical, romantic wedding!"I feel blessed to have my new son and his family as part of ours. "The bride herself wore a stunning wedding dress designed especially for her by Givenchy's creative director Riccardo Tisci.

      Guests at the nuptials included model Chrissy Teigen, who attended with husband John Legend, rapper Tyga and Kim's close friends La La Vazquez, Loren Ridinger and Malika.Kim's sisters Kourtney and Khloé and her half-sisters Kendall and Kylie were all in attendance, although her brother Rob Kardashian made the last-minute decision not to attend.Prior to tying the knot, Kim and Kanye threw a lavish pre-wedding party in Paris, where guests were treated to dinner and a performance by Lana Del Rey at the stunning Palace of Versailles.

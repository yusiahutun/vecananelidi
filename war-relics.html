    War Relics, Starfield Mission

    Vae Victis has given me the location of Kaiser, but expects I'll need some additional information to bring him home. He suggested I check in with Hadrian first. Hadrian will be waiting for you on Mars at Red Devils HQ. You have already visited this place during the &quot;Delivering Devils&quot; mission.

    05 May 2024, 00:00

    1. Report to Hadrian

    Vae Victis has given me the location of Kaiser, but expects I'll need some additional information to bring him home. He suggested I check in with Hadrian first.

    Hadrian will be waiting for you on Mars at Red Devils HQ. You have already visited this place during the "Delivering Devils" mission.

    Talk to Hadrian and inform her that you have information on her research team. You can keep the information about how you learned it to yourself, or reveal it to her, it doesn't really matter.

    2. Follow Hadrian

    Hadrian has something she thinks I'll need to recover Kaiser. I should follow her.

    Follow her and after a while you will receive the Schematic Catalogue: A-147-XW (KAISER) from her and more information on where to go and what to do.

    3. Search for Kaiser on Niira

    I've now got Kaiser's schematic catalogue and the password for his voice controls: "Nos Belli Machinis." Next stop: 1-Of-A-Kind Salvage on Niira.

    Travel to Narion System > Niira and land at 1-Of-A-Kind Salvage location.

    When you land on the planet then enter the building, find a man named Gel and talk to him. He will tell you where you can find Kaiser but will demand payment for more detailed information.

    4. Search for Kaiser in the mech fields / (Optional) Gather information on Kaiser's location from Gel / Proceed to the Syracuse

    Gel's given me two options - wander around Niira's dangerous mech fields in the hopes I stumble upon Kaiser, or pay him for the information.

    For additional information, you can pay him 1000 credits right away or persuade him to give you a discount and then it will cost you 675 credits.

    You will then learn that you should head to the crashed spaceship - The Syracuse.

    I know how to find Kaiser! Looks like I'm headed towards a nearby crashed spaceship - The Syracuse.

    5. Follow the beacon

    When you get close enough then you should hear the beacon.

    I hear the beacon! Kaiser can't be far now!

    Go inside the ship.

    6. Remove the creatures from Kaiser

    It looks like Kaiser's been infested with Heatleeches. I'll need to remove them... carefully.

    Just shoot the creatures.

    7. Speak to Kaiser

    I found Kaiser! Now I just need to give him the password - "Nos Belli Machinis" - to unlock his voice controls.

    Try to talk to him and give the correct password. Unfortunately, it will turn out that this is not enough because Kaiser has lost power.

    8. Find a MicroCell

    The Heatleeches have completely drained Kaiser's battery. He'll need a new one. He suggested I ask at the salvage yard if they have one.

    Return to the 1-Of-A-Kind Salvage and ask Gel about the Microcell.

    9. Purchase a MicroCell from Gel

    Gel stated he's got a MicroCell for sale... but also that they have the facilities to make them here. I should ask about that.

    You can buy a MicroCell from Gel but it costs as much as 12,000+ credits.

    10. (Optional) Talk to Gel about making MicroCells

    Talk to Gel and select "You said you've got the tools to make MicroCells here?". If you agree to his terms then he will mark the places where you should look for parts to make a MicroCell.

    11. (Optional) Recover MicroCell Shielding / Recover a MicroCell Power Source / Recover a MicroCell Conductor Array

    It sounds like I can either buy a MicroCell from Gel or manufacture my own. He gave me a couple sites where I should be able to recover the required components.

    Step outdoors and go to any three of the marked places. There you will find the parts you need.

    12. (Optional) Fabricate a MicroCell

    Return to the 1-Of-A-Kind Salvage and use the Industrial Workbench to craft a MicroCell.

    13. Return the MicroCell to Kaiser / Speak with Kaiser

    MicroCell acquired. Now I just need to hand it over to Kaiser.

    Just go back to Kaiser. He will want to complete his mission first and only then will he be willing to go to Mars with you.

    14. Follow Kaiser to the mission site / Speak to Kaiser

    Kaiser won't leave the planet unless I help him finish his current mission - eliminating the planet's final Xenoweapon, Unit 99 - so that's where we're headed.

    Follow Kaiser. Along the way you will be attacked by an alien creature that you must get rid of.

    Kaiser's got a bead on Unit 99, but there's a problem. I should speak to him.

    When you finally arrive at your destination then another problem arises. You have to choose whether to continue together with Kaiser or whether you want to do it alone.

    15. Clear the Ecliptic base

    The Ecliptic base is located at the bottom of the hill. Unit 99, the Siren creature is locked in a cage standing in the middle of the camp.

    If, as suggested by Kaiser, you decide to make a frontal attack then be prepared to fight several Eclipse soldiers and a creature simultaneously.

    Kaiser and I are going to clean out the Ecliptic Outpost and Unit 99 with it. Time to head in and raise hell!

    If you decide to do it on your own, you can use more subtle methods, such as sneak attacks as well as try to lock the beast in a cage.

    It seems Unit 99 has been captured by Ecliptic mercenaries. I'm going to head down to the outpost alone and see what's going on.

    15.1. (Optional) Find a way to seal Unit 99

    To do this, you should get undetected to the second floor of the container building and then access the computer terminal located there and select "Seal Specimen Cage". This way, when you are spotted by Eclipse soldiers, they won't be able to release the creature from its cage so you won't have to bother with it.

    16. Kill Unit 99

    Ecliptic's taken care of! Now to put down Unit 99!

    You simply have to kill the creature in any way you can. If you have managed to seal the cage the beast is in, this will be a trivial task. You can actually wait for Kaiser to do it for you.

    17. Follow Kaiser / Speak to Kaiser

    Unit 99 and the Ecliptic forces have been dealt with, but it seems Kaiser still has some business to take care of. I should stay close.

    After eliminating all the enemies and the beast, approach Kaiser who will be waiting for you near the cage and talk to him. Nothing more will stand in the way of you going to Mars.

    Kaiser's mission is now complete. I should talk to him about getting off this rock.

    18. Return to the Red Devils HQ

    Kaiser has agreed to accompany me back to Mars. He says he'll meet me back at my ship.

    Preferably,, use the fast travel option and return to the Red Devils HQ on Mars. This mission will end, you will receive 250 XP and 10,300 Credits, and the next mission will start - "Hostile Intelligence".

    With Kaiser back, Hadrian's ready to share the plan to stop the Terrormorphs. Sounds like we're heading for Londinion.

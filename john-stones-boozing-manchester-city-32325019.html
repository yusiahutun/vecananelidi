    England star's love of boozing exposed 'he'll drink bottles straight, no mixers'

    Manchester City star Kyle Walker revealed that his club and country team-mate John Stones has a love of boozing. The England defender has been team-mates with Stones for seven years with the Premier League champions as well as playing together for the Three Lions for 10 years.

    02 Mar 2024, 00:00

    Manchester City star Kyle Walker revealed that his club and country team-mate John Stones has a love of boozing.

    The England defender has been team-mates with Stones for seven years with the Premier League champions as well as playing together for the Three Lions for 10 years.

    The Treble winners enjoyed quite the celebrations after their historic achievement last year which saw Jack Grealish swigging a drink - or five - but it was the former Everton star that was shouted out by Walker who made an appearance on Rio Ferdinand's FIVE podcast this past week.

    The 33-year-old said: "John loves it [a party]. But he doesn’t drink all season" before adding that Stones has no use for mixers, instead preferring to drink straight from the bottle.

    "We went to Abu Dhabi, he had a little go then. But when he goes, it’s bottles. It’s no mixers, it’s just a bottle of tequila, a bottle of jaeger and he’ll just drink that straight. Just ‘give me the bottle’ and he drinks it," he continued.

    The five-time Premier League winner revealed how intense the drinking sessions get in trophy celebrations to which he said: "I absolutely love it. How high is the high when you’re celebrating with the lads? We stretch [nights out] to probably three days".

    Ferdinand's co-host Joel Beya then brought up Raheem Sterling's famous birthday video with a noticeably drunk Stones who began speaking in a Jamaican accent as Walker said the defender stopped drinking after that due to worries of injuries.

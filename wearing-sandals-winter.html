    Although it's perfectly natural to assume that sandals are only a wardrobe staple in the summer months, the most fashionable and stylish influencers and bloggers are making a case for the winter sandal, aka the 'wandal'.

    If you like trying different looks and mixing it up across the seasons, then follow our guide on how to look chic all year long in your favourite pair, regardless of the temperature.

    Cosy

    Take style inspiration from Pernille Teisbaek. The influencer kept cosy by choosing a pair that's fur-lined and wearing with neutral socks. To emulate the look keep the rest of your outfit casual - think jeans and your favourite puffer jacket to stay warm on the weekends.

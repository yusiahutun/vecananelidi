    As a Victoria's Secret Angel, Karlie Kloss is used to parading down catwalks wearing next to nothing.

    But now the 20-year-old model has taken things a step further and appeared wearing nothing at all in the latest issue of US Vogue magazine.

    Posing by a lake, wearing just a gold hair mask and white heels, Karlie stretched gracefully backwards, showing off her flawlessly athletic figure.

    Flawless: 20-year-old Karlie Kloss shows off her perfectly toned body in her first nude appearance for US Vogue

    Standing on a tree stump, the Chicago native held a pair of small silver dumbbells out in front of her, which highlighted her toned arms.

    Revealing an impossibly pert posterior, endless legs and a perfectly flat stomach, the photographs show why Kloss is in such high demand, having appeared as the face for Lanvin, Lacoste and Donna Karen in 2013 alone.

    In another picture, we see her sitting cross-legged wearing just a low cut white lace one-piece.

    Electrodes attached to each arm, the clotheshorse stares into the camera, her face lightly spritzed with water and her eyes highlighted with heavy black eyeliner.

    New age beauty: Karlie was photographed for an article about an upmarket detox experience at an Austrian health spa

    Photographed by industry legend Mario Testino, Karlie's various poses illustrate a piece about an upmarket but 'spartan' health spa in Austria.

    The natural beauty is certainly blessed with great genes, but she also sticks to a balanced diet and exercise plan to maintain her wonderful figure.

    She recently revealed details of her healthy eating regime which included her preference for a late breakfast - her favourite meal of the day.

    Style chameleon: Kloss is used to wearing some unusual outfits in her role as a Victoria's Secret model, but prefers to dress down when she's not on duty, as we can see from this picture of her, right, on Wednesday

    Cover girl: Katy Perry appears on the cover of the August issue of Vogue magazine

    'If I'm sleeping in my own bed in New York, I love to make just really yummy, clean, boring protein shakes with chocolate protein powder, almond milk, and banana or blackberries,' she told Bonappetit.com.

    'On days I don't have to work, I like to sleep in,' she added. 'I like to make a shake, go to the gym, then have breakfast around 2 - I'm all about breakfast, it's my favourite meal of the day. Mainly because there's so many yummy sweet options like cereals, yoghurt, pancakes, and crepes.

    'Egg whites are also a staple for me - they're a great source of protein and I can always have them no matter where in the world I'm working. I love my egg whites swimming in Cholula hot sauce or ketchup.'

    Karlie also advises drinking plenty of water, a balanced diet and sticking to a fitness regime.

    The August issue of US Vogue features Katy Perry on the cover, her first cover for the world's most famous fashion magazine.

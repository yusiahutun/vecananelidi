    Content creator Thee Pluto has opened up about how he found himself married to his beautiful wife, Felicity.

    The father of two narrated that he was open and told Felicity he had a kid with another woman when they started dating.

    When Thee Pluto introduced Felicity to his parents

    While appearing on Size 8 and DJ Mo's couple's show on TV 47, Pluto disclosed that the moment he found out Felicity was pregnant was the day he asked her to move in with him.

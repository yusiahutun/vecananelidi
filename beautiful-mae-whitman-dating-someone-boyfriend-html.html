    Mae Whitman’s Boyfriend and Dating History:

    Caption: Mae Whitman with Ex-boyfriend Landon Pigg at the ATX Television Festival.

    Source: whatchamacallitreviews.blogspot.com

    According to Heavy.com, Mae was once dating Landon Pigg who is also a singer/songwriter. He was the one who helped her to write the songs she sang on the show. In 2014, to Glamour.com, she revealed that she still enjoyed a good friendship with Mae. In the interview, she referred him as her favorite guest star on the show Parenthood. Landon guest starred on Parenthood for a year.

    In the same interview, she chose to keep her dating life a secret. When she was asked whether she was dating anyone, she said:

     "I am. I'm pretty secret about that stuff, I guess. I never really care, but I'm pretty secretive. It's a great relationship."

    And when she was asked what she would have done had she not decided to act, she said:

    I was talking about this with my boyfriend. I have the weirdest answers. It would be either working at a zoo helping the animals or as a park ranger. I love being outside. I could also totally see myself giving museum tours. I love talking to people and having them ask questions and getting discussions going about art. What would you do?

    Not only that, the Glamour interviewer also asked her about her dating preference. To the question "Do you have a type?", she responded:

    It's constantly changing. I just like people. I like everyone. I see so much wonderful potential in everyone that I meet, and there's always something to learn from. I think girls are attractive, I think boys are attractive, I think old people are attractive, I think young people are—I appreciate everything and everyone.  

    Nevertheless, she was also rumored to be dating Haley Joel Osment in 2002. But it turns out he was just her best friend and not a boyfriend.

    Mae Whitman’s Pregnancy Rumor?

    In 2015, Mae shared her side of the story to Teen Vouge on the occasion of Nationa Teen Pregnancy Prevention Month.

    She became pregnant at the age of 17 and went to Planned Parenthood for help. She got explained what it is like having a child and how it changes one person’s whole life. All that helped her in deciding that she was not ready to become pregnant and have a child.

    She shared another of her story in an interview with People (2015) about her struggle while in school. She said she was considered nothing more than “Designated Ugly Fat Friend” in her high school. She said,

    "High school is terrible. It's really mean. I was bullied all through school. It was awful. I was a tomboy and nerdy." 

    This experience might have driven her into the way of weight loss, and here she is now, more beautiful than life.

    Mae Whitman’s Short Bio:

    Mae Whitman (age - 28) was born on June 9, 1988, in California, U.S. She was enrolled in a private school in Los Angeles. She first started acting with a voice-over job for Tyson Chicken commercial at the age of three. After about three years, she made her first debut in a movie called “When a Man Loves a Woman” alongside Meg Ryan.

    She started playing a recurring role on Arrested Development from 2004 and appeared in FX Network’s Thief in 2006. In 2009, Mae got a regular role in NBC’s version of Ron Howard classic Parenthood for which she is widely famous for. Recently, she can be seen in a comedy drama called 12 March 2016. 

    She is also famous for portraying the character Bianca Piper, an unpopular but smart high school student who is labeled as the ugly/fat friend in her friend circle, in the movie called The Duff (2015). The story of the movie somewhat similar to her own struggle in high school. Currently,

    Her recent notable work credentials include movies such as Rock Dog (2016), Drawn Of The Dead (2016), and Operator (2016), and television series such as Family Guy (2008-2016), Parenthood (2010-2015), Teenange Mutant Ninja Turtle (2012-present, voice), Dragons: Riders of Berk (2012-present), and DC Super Hero Girls: Super Hero High (2016).

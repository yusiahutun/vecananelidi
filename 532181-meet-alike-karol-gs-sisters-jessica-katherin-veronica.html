But aside from her career, the star has generated interest due to her look-alike siblings. But who are they? And who are Karol G's sisters?

    Celebrities' lives generate interest from the public for various reasons. This narrative is particularly true for the sisters of Colombian singer-songwriter Karol G, who have garnered significant attention due to their resemblances and close-knit relationship with their famous sister.

    Who is Karol G?

    Karol G, whose real name is Carolina Giraldo Navarro, is a renowned Colombian singer and songwriter. She has a successful career, which has seen her release four studio albums, two mixtapes and over 70 singles (at the time of writing). Karol G has received several awards, including five Latin Grammy Awards and four Billboard Music Awards.

    How many sisters does Karol G have? The singer has three sisters: two full sisters and one half-sister. Karol G and her full siblings have a close bond and often spend time together.     Verónica is the oldest sister in the Giraldo family. She was born on 11 September 1987 in Medellín, Colombia.     Katherin Giraldo

    Kathetin is a half-sister to Karol G and was born from a relationship between Guillermo Giraldo and her now-deceased mother. Based on her social media, Katherin is a stylist, professional colourist and model. She was reportedly born on 19 December 1995. According to the American Post, despite having ties to the Giraldo family, Katherin is estranged from them.

    Are Karol G. and Becky G. related?

    No, they are not. Despite sharing the same last name (which is a coincidence), Karol G and Becky G are not related by blood. They are both famous singers and have collaborated on some songs, such as MAMIII, but they have different backgrounds and ethnicities. Karol G, real name Carolina Giraldo Navarro, is Colombian, while Becky G, real name Rebbeca Marie Gomez, is American.

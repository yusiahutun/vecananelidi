    I've had grays since I was 21, and it's been a constant battle to keep them in check between appointments without going broke. At first, I'd notice one or two pop up here and there, but now it feels like they're multiplying overnight. I'm not sure if it's hereditary, but as Samantha Jones once said: “Pluck one gray hair out, three more will come to its funeral” — and she couldn't have been more right.

    I'll be 30 in April, and the struggle to hide my grays is still very real. I get my hair dyed once every six weeks at the salon, but that doesn't always cut it. So, as a sneaky trick that I'll probably use until the day I decide to go full-on silver fox, I rely on the TreSemmé Root Touch-Up Temporary Hair Color Spray to keep my grays under control between salon appointments.

    At just $7 a bottle, the magic this touch-up spray works is nothing short of amazing. Its targeted spray nozzle instantly covers my grays, giving me a natural-looking color that blends right in with my dark roots. I spray it in the areas where I have grays, wait for it to dry (about one minute), give it a quick brush through, and I'm out the door. The spray is available in four shades, including black, dark blonde, light brown, and dark brown.

    It lasts throughout the day and doesn't fade out until my next wash, making it an affordable and convenient solution for hiding those silver strands that stubbornly peek through. (Since I have dark hair, you can really see the contrast when the grays start to show.) In addition to the spray, I also use the IGK Color Depositing Conditioning Hair Mask to keep my hair color from becoming brassy between visits.

    More than 3,200 Amazon shoppers rely on the TreSemmé Root Touch-Up Temporary Hair Color Spray for their grays, too. One shopper with “thin hair on top” said the spray helps them look like they “have a lot more hair” and “keeps gray roots away,” too. “My grays grow in quicker than I can keep up, this spray is a lifesaver! It covers grays perfectly in between colorings,” another user wrote.

    Not only is this spray a go-to for grays, but many Amazon reviewers praise it for its ability to give the illusion of fuller hair by covering up thinning spots or patches. One 31-year-old reviewer said they use it to get a "fuller look" for messy buns and ponytails. Another reviewer noted that the spray “looks natural and doesn’t rub off.” “I have thinning hair, so I have used root touch up products for years,” they wrote. “TreSemmé is by far the best I have used for the price.”

    Whether you're fighting grays, looking to cover up thinning spots, or just want a quick touch-up between salon appointments, the TreSemmé Root Touch-Up Temporary Hair Color Spray is a quick and cost-effective solution.

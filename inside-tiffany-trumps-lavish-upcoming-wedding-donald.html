    Inside Tiffany Trumps lavish upcoming wedding: Donald Trump and Marla Maples daughter will wed

    The socialite will turn 29 years old just one month before saying I do, while Boulos will turn 25 years old this August. The wedding will also take place just days after the US midterm elections.

    30 Sep 2024, 00:00
    Tiffany has been spotted scouting out wedding locations in Mykonos, Greece, where she reportedly met her billionaire heir fiancé Boulos at Lindsay Lohan’s beach club. While Donald’s daughter planned on getting married in multiple locations, according to Vanity Fair – including in Europe this summer – she will now reportedly hold her main wedding in the United States on November 12.

    The socialite will turn 29 years old just one month before saying “I do”, while Boulos will turn 25 years old this August. The wedding will also take place just days after the US’ midterm elections.

    Who’s hosting the lavish bash?

    Tiffany’s mum Maples will oversee the event with her ex-husband Donald, British media reported. The latter certainly has some experience, given that he’s been married three times.

    There’s no word yet on what Tiffany’s wedding dress will look like, but if her mother’s gown is anything to go by, it will be striking. In looking back at how dad did it, Donald and Maples exchanged vows at New York’s Plaza Hotel – which Donald owned at one time – and the bride wore a silk Carolina Herrera gown with sleeves plus a tiara estimated to be worth US$2 million, according to The Knot. Guests dined on caviar and tucked into a seven-tier cake.

    Donald married his first wife Ivana in a secret ceremony in 1977 before they divorced in 1992 and she wed her fourth husband – the late Rossano Rubicondi – in 2008 at Donald’s Mar-a-Lago club. Ivana and Rubicondi divorced in 2009.

    Donald married Barron Trump’s mother, Melania, in 2005 at the Episcopal Church of Bethesda by the Sea in Palm Beach, Florida then they enjoyed a reception at Mar-a-Lago.

    “There has been a lot of planning and a lot of staging. This is Tiffany’s big moment, and it will be lavish. It is going to be a huge and beautiful affair,” a source told British media.

    How big will the celebration be?

    There will be over 500 guests to watch the couple tie the knot, according to British media.

    What venue has Tiffany chosen?

    Tiffany’s wedding photos are sure to be spectacular. The second daughter of Donald is getting hitched at his famous Mar-a-Lago resort, which has boasted celebrity members of its private club as well as high-profile stars as guests.

    But not everyone has the option of getting married at Mar-a-Lago. To host a wedding at the property, you must be sponsored by a member. The Palm Beach, Florida club costs US$200,000 to join plus US$14,000 in annual fees.

    Wedding guests will gain access to “world-class amenities” including the “finest signature European indoor and terrace dining”, an oceanfront beach club and spa, six championship tennis courts and more. The venue is a short drive from Miami, where Boulos and Tiffany moved to after her dad’s one-term presidency was over.

    It also happens to now be the main home of Donald, Melania and Barron after their time in the White House. Before Donald met his third wife, he lived at Mar-a-Lago from 1985 to 1995.

    According to its website, Mar-a-Lago is the only place in the world to “attain the prestigious six-star Diamond Award from the American Academy of Hospitality Sciences”. It adds, “The Trump Organization has invested millions of dollars in restoring and upgrading this ‘Jewel of Palm Beach’.”

    Its offerings include descriptions as the “most coveted entertaining space”, with its white and gold ballroom and the sumptuous Donald J. Trump grand ballroom which was built in 2005. At 6,100 metres (20,000 feet), the ballroom is the largest on the island. “The interior is in a Louis XIV gold and crystal finish that is one of the finest spaces of its kind in the country,” the website reads.

    The wedding and events page suggests “an incredible cocktail party under the stars poolside”, which all adds up to make quite the soirée for Miss Trump.

    What’s Mar-a-Lago’s background?

    “Mar-a-Lago” is Spanish for ocean to lake. The 114-room villa is situated between the Atlantic Ocean and Lake Worth and was built in 1927 for American businesswoman and socialite Marjorie Merriweather Post (then Mrs E.F. Hutton) who married four times in her lifetime. The property was willed to the federal government as a presidential retreat in 1973, but the government gave it up due to maintenance issues. In 1985, Donald bought the property for US$10 million. Mar-a-Lago is on what’s now the “most valuable parcel of land anywhere in Florida”, according to the venue website.

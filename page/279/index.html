Disco Donnie Presents Acquires Long-Running Texas EDM Festival Lights All Night

DALLAS (CelebrityAccess) — EDM festival and event promoter Disco Donnie Presents announced the acquisition of the Texas music festival Lights All Night (LAN). The Lights All Night festival will join Disco Donnie’s existing portfolio of regional music events that already includes Ubbi Dubbi and Freaky Deaky.

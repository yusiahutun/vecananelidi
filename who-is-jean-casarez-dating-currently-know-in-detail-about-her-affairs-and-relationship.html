    Jean Casarez's Career Highlights: What is she up to now?

    Casarez is working as New York City-based news correspondent for CNN and its sister network HLN. She also served as a substitute host in the show, Primetime Justice occasionally.

    Prior to CNN, she worked at Court TV which is now known as TruTV as a news correspondent. During her service at the network, she covered many stories like O.J. Simpson's kidnapping trial, the murder of Jon Benet Ramsey the civil rights trial of Edgar Ray Killen, Kobe Bryant, the murder of Jon Benet Ramsey and much more.

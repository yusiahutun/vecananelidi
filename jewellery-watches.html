    Best jewellery at Cannes 2023

    Julia Garner completed her head-to-toe Gucci ensemble the actress wore a striking green gown to the premiere of Asteroid with a pair of spectacular white gold earrings with emeralds and diamonds, as well as a mint tourmaline and diamond ring, from Gucci's High Jewellery collection.

    10 Feb 2024, 00:00
    PATRICIA DE MELO MOREIRA//Getty Images

    Julia Garner

    Lionel Hahn//Getty Images

    Julia Garner completed her head-to-toe Gucci ensemble – the actress wore a striking green gown to the premiere of Asteroid – with a pair of spectacular white gold earrings with emeralds and diamonds, as well as a mint tourmaline and diamond ring, from Gucci's High Jewellery collection.

    Natalie Portman

    LOIC VENANCE//Getty Images

    Natalie Portman paired her shimmering Dior gown with a deep blue sapphire and diamond necklace from the Chopard Red Carpet collection.

    Araya Hargate

    Andreas Rentz//Getty Images

    Araya Hargate's Bulgari Serpenti necklaces and stackable bracelets, rings and earrings were a look to remember from this year's Cannes Film Festival. She wore not one, but two Serpenti necklaces (one in white gold and one in pink gold), each featuring two pear brilliant-cut diamonds and pavé diamonds.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Jennifer Lawrence

    Pascal Le Segretain//Getty Images

    Jennifer Lawrence added a touch of sparkle to her premiere look with an asymmetric necklace made of white gold and diamonds by Dior Joaillerie.

    Gigi Hadid

    PATRICIA DE MELO MOREIRA//Getty Images

    Gigi Hadid stole the show at the Firebrand premiere, wearing Messika high jewellery designs with her Zac Posen dress. Her jewels were from the brand's 'Paris est une Fête' collection, including the Roaring Diamonds necklace and matching bracelet; the Illusionnistes ear clip; the timeless Glam’Azone ring; the Gloria double ring; and an exceptional pink and yellow diamond Toi&Moi heart-shaped ring.

    Coco Rocha

    Samir Hussein//Getty Images

    Coco Rocha wore a sculptural necklace and earrings set – from Chaumet's 'Ondes & Merveilles' collection – to the premiere of Club Zero. The pieces featured a shimmering string of ruby stones and bright diamonds, which contrasted together perfectly.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Irina Shayk

    Marc Piasecki//Getty Images

    Irina Shayk wore another impressive piece from Messika on the red carpet this year: the high jewellery Akh-Ba-Ka necklace, arguably one of Messika’s most special pieces to date. This remarkable 33-carat diamond necklace is inspired by ancient Egyptian mythology and style, set in diamonds and white gold.

    Laura Harrier

    Gisela Schober//Getty Images

    Laura Harrier let her jewellery do all the talking at Cannes, with Bulgari's high jewellery Serpenti earrings. The pair were crafted from white gold with onyx, two pear rubellites, six marquise brilliant-cut diamonds, 16 fancy step-cut diamonds, two round brilliant-cut diamonds and pavé-set diamonds.

    Kirsten Dunst

    Gareth Cattermole//Getty Images

    Kirsten Dunst dipped into the archives for her 2023 Cannes appearance, pairing her timeless vintage Chanel gown with an impressive Cartier piece from 2004. The actress, who attended the Killers Of The Flower Moon premiere, wore a necklace featuring hundreds of diamonds set in platinum.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Alessandra Ambrosio

    Marc Piasecki//Getty Images

    Alessandra Ambrosio added to the shimmer of her hooded Elie Saab dress – from the brand's autumn/winter 2005 couture collection – with the show-stopping Pomellato wrap-around Skyline necklace and co-ordinating earrings. Inspired by Milan’s architectural landscape, the asymmetric necklace is handset with an impressive selection of 128 rare, bezel-cut spinels that capture the sunset silhouettes of the Milanese sky.

    Elle Fanning

    Marc Piasecki//Getty Images

    Elle Fanning perfectly paired her fairy-tale Alexander McQueen gown with a beaded Cartier choker, for the opening ceremony and premiere of Jeanne du Barry.

    Soo Joo Park

    Marc Piasecki//Getty Images

    Soo Joo Park added a splash of colour to her beaded Saint Laurent dress with statement-making turquoise earrings by Chopard, at the premiere of Monster.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Naomi Campbell

    Samir Hussein//Getty Images

    Naomi Campbell was a shining example of chic event dressing, matching her silver sequinned Celine look with diamond chandelier earrings by Chopard.

    Uma Thurman

    Mondadori Portfolio//Getty Images

    Uma Thurman wore a beautiful choker necklace with matching earrings, both by Chopard, on the red carpet – the perfect accompaniments to her blush pink Dior gown and scarlet opera coat.

    Pom Klementieff

    Lionel Hahn//Getty Images

    Pom Klementieff added to the drama of her Versace look with an impressive Cartier diamond necklace and matching earrings.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Viola Davis

    Pascal Le Segretain//Getty Images

    Viola Davis proved that sometimes less is more when it comes to jewellery. The actress styled her breathtaking Valentino haute couture gown with circular Chopard earrings.

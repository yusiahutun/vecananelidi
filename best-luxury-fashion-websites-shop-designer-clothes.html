    The 8 Best Luxury Fashion Websites to Shop for Designer Clothes

    12 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Shopping for clothes online is fun, and can even be therapeutic. Whether you're looking to treat yourself, or if you’d just like to browse through the latest runway collections as a fan of high-end fashion, luxury fashion websites are where you should go.

    Think of Hermès’ classic Birkin bags or Louis Vuitton’s iconic Neverfull Tote—despite the fact that they’ve been around for decades and their designs have stayed the same, they are still wildly popular and highly sought after. So, you can think of designer wear as an investment—because, technically, it is.

    Here are some of the best luxury fashion websites that ship worldwide.

    1. Net-A-Porter

    Founded in 2000 in London, Net-A-Porter is one of several mainstream luxury fashion marketplaces on this list, and with good reason. The website boasts over 800 designer brands, 200 premium beauty brands, and ships to more than 170 countries.

    New items also arrive on the website thrice a week, and bestsellers are restocked frequently. So, you can basically browse the Net-A-Porter website all day long and go through thousands of luxury clothes, bags, shoes, and accessories until you find your favorite one.

    It is also known for its impressive express shipping service, where customers in Manhattan, London, and Hong Kong are able to receive their orders on the same day they purchased them.

    2. Farfetch

    Founded and owned by Portuguese billionaire José Neves, Farfetch operates from the UK and ships over 1,300 luxury designer brands to more than 170 countries.

    To give you an idea of how reputable Farfetch is, the website was selected as the exclusive online retailer for the summer collection of Rihanna’s fashion line, FENTY.

    The company has also recently launched a handbag resale service in the US called Farfetch Second Life, where users are able to sell preloved designer handbags on the site for Farfetch store credit. If you’re a fan of second-hand pieces, check out our list of these online thrift shops.

    If you are unhappy with your items, you can also take advantage of Farfetch’s returns service, which is completely free.

    3. MyTheresa

    MyTheresa is also one of the major high-end online marketplaces alongside Farfetch and Net-A-Porter. With over 800 employees and a presence of over thirty years in the industry, MyTheresa has partnered with renowned female fashion designers for the launch of online exclusive capsule collections, including Stella McCartney, Tory Burch, and Gabriela Hearst.

    Orders from the website ship in three days, and usually come from its logistics center in Munich.

    4. Estro

    Estro is the biggest luxury designer outlet in Australia, and now ships worldwide. The website stocks a wide range of premier men’s and women’s brands from Europe, including Gucci, Prada, Versace, Fendi, and Givenchy.

    All prices shown online are in Australian Dollars (AUD), so be sure to have a currency converter handy when you’re browsing through your favorite designer clothes.

    On Estro, you can also pay with Zip and Afterpay, which are buy-now-pay-later (BNPL) services that allow you to own the desired item first, and then pay for it in installments. If you're curious about this process, check out some of the most reliable BNPL services to help with your next purchase.

    5. Ssense

    Based in Montreal, Ssense has a global presence in more than 150 countries, and the website records over 76 million hits every month. On top of designer brands that we’re familiar with, Ssense also focuses on designer streetwear and avant-garde brands, such as Off-White, Acne Studios, and Comme des Garçons.

    The website’s interface is probably the cleanest and simplest one on this list. Clothes and accessories are categorized under menswear, womenswear, and "everything else." After clicking on any of them, you will then be presented with a neat, long list of designer brands that you can click and explore with ease.

    6. Matchesfashion

    Matchesfashion has three physical stores in London, but its online marketplace is equally extensive. The website stocks high-end clothing and accessories from more than 650 established and up-and-coming designers.

    The company also has a dedicated team of stylists that are ready to provide you with fashion advice suited to your personal style. Orders from London can enjoy same-day delivery.

    7. The Outnet

    The Outnet is owned by Net-A-Porter, and it is one of few luxury fashion websites that offer exceptional discounts on the best designer pieces. You can get up to 50 to 70 percent off on brands, such as Valentino and Dolce and Gabbana. If you're a first-time shopper, you can also get 15 percent off your first order.

    So, why is The Outnet able to offer such large volumes of top-tier brands at competitive prices? This is because its buying team in London purchases 90 percent of the stock directly from designer brands.

    If you're overwhelmed by the choices and not sure what to wear, simply navigate to the Editorial tab on the top right corner. Here, you can explore fashion ideas from seasoned professionals in the industry.

    8. Moda Operandi

    Co-founded by former Vogue editor, Lauren Santo Domingo, Moda Operandi brings to its website the trendiest designer pieces, jewelry, and even home decor.

    The fashion website also runs trunk shows, in which designers directly showcase their work exclusively to a selected crowd—in this case, customers on the site. This means that you are able to get the first glimpse at a designer’s latest collection and buy them before anyone else.

    On top of that, you are also able to preorder upcoming seasonal collections from well-known designer brands, weeks before they make their debut in stores. Pay a deposit to place an order, and it will be shipped as soon as it arrives on the site.

    Get Quality Clothing on Luxury Fashion Websites

    Shopping on luxury fashion websites can be an exciting experience, and buying your first designer item is always memorable.

    If you are worried about whether or not the items on the above websites are authentic, don’t worry—these websites work closely with fashion houses to secure legitimate clothing items and accessories for you. Happy shopping!

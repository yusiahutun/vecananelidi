    They have been happily married since 2016, having sparked up a romance two years before.

    And Liberty Ross looked more in love than ever as she cosied up to billionaire husband Jimmy Iovine on a lunch date in Notting Hill, London on Tuesday. 

    The model, 44, couldn't stop laughing with the record producer, 70, as they explored the city.

    She looked effortlessly chic in a blue pinstriped midi dress, teamed with beige trainers.

    Liberty opted for a natural makeup look underneath a pair of stylish sunglasses, while her raven tresses were scraped back in a bun.

    Liberty shot to stardom as an actress and model while her husband achieved success as the co-founder of Interscope Records and Beats Electronics.

    Lovine first made waves in the music industry as a record engineer and then producer, working alongside such classic artists as John Lennon, Bruce Springsteen, Tom Petty, Patti Smith and U2.

    He went on to be the co-founder of Interscope Records. 

    In 2008, Iovine teamed up with Dr. Dre to co-found Beats By Dr. Dre, a headphones brand that eventually captured 20 percent market share of the headphones industry within four years.

    Apple Inc. went on to acquire Beats Electronics for the dup for $3 billion in 2014, according to Variety. 

    The sale also resulted in Iovine landing a job at Apple to help in the creation of Apple Music.

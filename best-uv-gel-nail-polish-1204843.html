    How to Select the Best UV Gel Nail Polish?

    Here are the aspects that you need to consider before purchasing nail polishes- 

    Color: Always make sure that you pick a nail polish color or shade that suits your style. You can pick one depending on your skin tone and fashion persona. Neutral colors look great on normal to medium skin tones, whereas if you possess a dark complexion, choose a cool tone color. For lighter complexion, it is observed that warm and bright colors look the best. 

    Consistency: Always check the consistency of the nail polish that you choose to purchase. This will help you predict your application session. Some gel nail polishes have thicker consistency, whereas some possess runny consistency. A nail polish that is thick needs more care and attention. On the other hand, it is easier to stroke your nails when the consistency tends to be thin. It is advisable to vouch for nail polish that has neither too thick nor too runny a consistency. Remember! Thicker the nail polish longer the drying time.  

    Drying Duration: After checking the consistency of the nail polish, pay heed to the drying duration of the nail polish. Generally, all nail polishes require a base coat that takes 30 seconds under UV or LED light to cure. After that, your nail polish should dry completely for another 60 seconds. To seal off your shiny look, apply a top coat and then cure it under the lamp for more than 60 seconds. To sum up, your nail polish should dry in a span of 3 minutes. 

    Chip-resistant: Make sure that your nail polish contains natural resins and dries quickly under the UV lamp. Any nail polish that takes a longer time to cure should be avoided as it may result in chipping. 

    Long-lasting: The nail polish should stay on your nails at least for 21 to 28 days. So, pick a nail polish that is long wearing, and doesn’t fade or chip. You must choose toxic-free and odorless nail polishes with a good base and top coat. 

    You must be figuring out the right method of applying the best UV gel nail polishes. Well, you need nothing but patience and of course a UV or LED lamp. Roll your eyes below to understand the correct method of application.

    How to Apply UV Gel Nail Polish?

    • Step 1: Always ensure that your nails are dry and oil-free before kickstarting your nail painting session. 
    • Step 2: Push back the cuticle gently and remove excess skin if any. 
    • Step 3: File the edges of your nails and shape them as desired. 
    • Step 4: Buff the surface of your nail gently. 
    • Step 5: Use a nail brush and clean your nails and the area around them. 
    • Step 6: Apply a nail dehydrator and then apply a base coat. Cure it under the UV lamp, minimum of 30 and a maximum of 60 seconds. 
    • Step 7: Apply a thin layer of UV gel polish and cure it under the UV or LED lamp for another 60 seconds. 
    • Step 8: Apply the second layer of nail polish and let it dry under the lamp again for 60 seconds. It is normal for the gel polish to feel sticky even after curing. Wait for some time and do not wipe it off immediately. To avoid chipping, the thinner the layer, the better the results. 
    • Step 9: After your nail polish is dried completely, apply a thin layer of top coat. Make sure you cap the edges of your nails. 
    • Step 10: Cure your nails once again under the UV lamp for 60 seconds and let them dry completely.

    FAQ's

    Which is the best UV LED gel polish?

    MEFA, MEMEDA, and BORN PRETTY are the three brands that have introduced the best UV LED gel polish.

    Is UV or LED better for gel nails?

    LED lamps are better for gel nails as they help cure your nails faster.

    Is the UV light for gel nails harmful?

    Excessive or long-term use can damage your nails, cuticles, and nailbeds adversely.

    How to dry gel nail polish without UV light?

    Dip your nails in ice-cold water to let your gel nail polish dry without UV light.

    How to remove UV gel nail polish?

    Dip a cotton ball into acetone and wrap your nails around it for a few minutes. This will soften your gel nail polish and ease the removal process.

    Is UV gel nail polish bad for your nails?

    Excessive usage of gel nail polish can make your nails brittle. They can also peel and crack the surface of your nail.

    Can you use gel nail polish without UV light?

    Yes, but ensure that your nail polish has a formula that doesn't require a UV or LED light to dry.

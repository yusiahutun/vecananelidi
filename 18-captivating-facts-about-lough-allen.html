    Lough Allen is located in County Leitrim, Ireland. It is situated in the northwest part of the county, near the border with County Sligo.

    Lough Allen covers an area of approximately 8,000 acres, making it one of the largest lakes in Ireland. It also has a maximum depth of around 47 meters.

    Jon B: Biography

    He was born on 11th of November,1974, in Providence, Rhode Island. He is the second youngest of three siblings whose names are Deborah, his older sister and Kelvin, his younger brother. Incidentally, his siblings are both into music. His father David Buck, is a professor of music at Cal State while his mom Linda Buck, is a concert pianist. His demographic is Caucasian but he was raised in the African-American community in Pasadena, California and he visited a black church while growing up.

    Jon B: Career

    Having a musical background, his music career was inspired by some music albums he found in his grandparents’ record box. Among his best artists at that time were Babyface, Michael Jackson, Marvin Gaye, Duran Duran, Prince and Sade, among others. He started writing his own songs mostly during summer and after his high school year.He was able to produce and record about 40 marvelous songs including his debut album, Bonafide which became a huge hit at the time. Bonafide which was released on the Epic Records subsidiary 550 Music, was also the first recording released on Tracey Edmonds’ record label Yab Yum Record.

    Early Life

    Nicole Eggert was born on January 13, 1972, in Glendale, California.

    Child Star

    Eggert began her acting career at the age of five when she appeared in a Johnson & Johnson shampoo commercial.

    Baywatch Fame

    Nicole Eggert is most recognized for her role as Summer Quinn in the popular TV series Baywatch, which aired from 1992 to 1994.

    In 1989, Eggert won the Young Artist Award for Best Young Actress in a Television Comedy Series for her role in Charles in Charge.

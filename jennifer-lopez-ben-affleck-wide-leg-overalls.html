    Jennifer Lopez Swaps Her Wide-Leg Jeans for Wide-Leg Overalls

    16 Aug 2024, 00:00

    It’s no secret that Jennifer Lopez is a fan of baggy denim. While her affection for the material often manifests by way of a pair of wide-leg jeans, she recently put a cozy and casual twist on the fad by stepping out in wide-leg overalls.

    Out with husband Ben Affleck in Los Angeles on Sunday, the “Can’t Get Enough” singer looked effortlessly cool in oversize stonewash denim overalls from R13, paired with a waffle knit white top and a long creamy cable knit cardigan. She kept the cozy vibes going with her footwear, opting for what appears to be a caramel iteration of Ugg’s mini platform boots, and carried a small croc-embossed Hermès Birkin bag in blood orange. To finish off her ensemble, she accessorized with her signature hoop earrings.

    Affleck also went for a low-key outfit in a black-and-white plaid shearling-lined shacket layered over a black and dark gray T-shirt, as well as a pair of black pants and black and blue Nike sneakers.

    BACKGRID

    Fresh off the release of her ninth studio album, This Is Me…Now, Lopez recently opened up about how Affleck supported her creative process. “When Ben and I got back together, it was just like, ‘I want to make music again, I want to get back in the studio,’” she said in a press conference. “I was very, very inspired.”

    She added, “It ended this 20-year journey about a lot of questions that I had about love and being myself, a hopeless romantic, and what it means to really enter into a kind of healthier, more self-accepting phase for myself. Now what will happen from that, I hope, will be even more wonderful things that I have been so privileged to live up until this point.”

    Birthday invite message for adults

    Birthday invitation wording sets the mood for your birthday party. It will help your guest know what is expected of them on your big day. The following messages will help you get started:

    Son's birthday invitation message for WhatsApp

    A son's big day is special to the parents. Your child might be young to plan the party, but you can make it worthwhile for him. Take many pictures that can remind him of the big day. Here are invitation messages you can share with your friends:

    • Our son is turning a year older, and the day will be incomplete without you. You are dearly welcome to celebrate the big day with us.
    • We would be happy if you graced our son's big day with us. We are hoping to see you on that day.
    • You are a close family member; my son's big day will not be the same again without you gracing it. So come and make it exciting.
    • My family has organized a party to celebrate our son's birthday. You are invited to make it more glamorous.
    • We invite you to join our son's birthday party, which is full of fun, laughter, food and a delicious cake.
    • We sincerely request your presence at our son's birthday dinner. Hoping to see you soon.
    • Our son will be turning five years in a week. You are invited to celebrate with us on (date) at our place.
    • Our son's big day is around the corner. We will be so happy if you joined us for the celebration.
    • We dearly request that you be one of our guests as we mark our son's new year on (date).

    Birthday invite message for friends

    Birthdays are times to celebrate. Some do it by eating and drinking, listening to music or hanging with friends. You can invite your friends by sharing the following simple messages to their WhatsApp:

    • As we come together to enjoy (name) birthday, we will be honoured by your presence. So please come and be part of the fun-filled day.
    • The joy of a special birthday celebration is multiplied when our loved ones are present. Therefore, you can't afford to miss (name) birthday party.
    • Honouring our invite to (name) birthday party is the ultimate gift. Therefore, we cordially invite you to celebrate this great day with us.
    • Your presence at (name) birthday party will bring us infinite joy. So, kindly grace the occasion with your presence.
    • On (date) at (location and time), we will be gathered together to shower (name) with birthday blessings as he turns a year older. See you there.
    • As (name) is turning a year older, we couldn't think of a better person to share the joy and blessing with. We hope to see you soon.
    • Sweets, treats, cake and unending fun, will make (name) a birthday party. We can't wait to see you join us and be part of it all.
    • This WhatsApp message is to invite you to my birthday party officially. I would be glad to see you there.

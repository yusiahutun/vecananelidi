The beautiful female Demon Slayer characters make the entire series endearing, fun, and exciting.

    Japanese manga series and anime became popular in the early 1990s and peaked at the beginning of the 20th century. Demon Slayer is one of the most popular manga series. Since its first season, the series has been largely praised for its concrete world-building and how it perfectly humanizes its human and demon characters.

    1. Nezuko Kamado

    Nezuko is the deuteragonist and main female protagonist of the manga series. She is the younger sister of Tanjiro Kamado. Nezuko appears as a fair-skinned young girl with long dark brown hair that reaches just below her waist.

    In terms of her demon abilities, she can manipulate her physical form and age to a certain extent. Nezuko possesses extraordinary regeneration abilities that are common to demons. However, at full potential, she can solidify and manipulate her blood at will.

    2. Shinobu Kocho

    Shinobu is one of the female Demon Slayer characters in season 2. She is the Insect Pillar of the Demon Slayer Corps in the series. Kocho is a beautiful young lady who doesn't fail to smile even in the toughest times.

    She is one of the only few demon slayers incapable of cutting the necks of the demons. The butterfly-clad fighter takes out demons with unique innovation and agility. She has cemented her position as a vital player in the Demon Slayer universe.

    3. Kanao Tsuyuri

    Kanao is the adoptive sister of Kanae Kocho and Shinobu Kocho. She is depicted as a quiet girl with a tragic past. Despite her young age, Kanao is a high-ranking member of the Demon Slayer Corps. She was trained since childhood by her sisters Shinobu and Kanae in the arts of combat.

    As a result, she possesses superhuman physical traits and martial prowess that singles her out as a possible future Hashira. Like all Demon Slayers, she possesses overwhelming fighting power thanks to the total concentration breathing technique.

    4. Mitsuri Kanroji

    Who is the female Hashira in Demon Slayer? Mitsuri Kanroji is arguably one of the more likable Hashira or Pillars in the manga series. In terms of her demon abilities, Mitsuri is a very powerful and skilled combatant.

    She can make her muscles eight times denser than an average human and thus sustain powerful attacks from opponents. Additionally, Mitsuri can move at great speed and has enhanced flexibility, stamina, and endurance. Like her Demon Slayer anime comrades, she has mastered the Love Breathing technique, which enables her to wield her weapon of choice.

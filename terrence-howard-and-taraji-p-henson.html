    About

    Taraji P. Henson is a 53 year old American Actress. Born Taraji Penda Henson on 11th September, 1970 in Washington, District of Columbia, USA, she is famous for her roles as Yvette in the 2001 coming-of-age hood comedy-drama film Baby Boy, Shug in the 2005 independent musical drama film Hustle & Flow, Queenie in 2008 romantic fantasy drama film The Curious Case of Benjamin Button, and Cookie Lyon in the Fox musical drama series Empire in a career that spans 1995–present and 1992–present. Her zodiac sign is Virgo.

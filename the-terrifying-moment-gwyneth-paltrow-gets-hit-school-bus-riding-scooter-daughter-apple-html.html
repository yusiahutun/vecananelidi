    It seems Gwyneth Paltrow narrowly escaped disaster while riding a scooter through the streets of LA with her daughter on Wednesday.

    The 40-year-old was caught on camera pulling out in front of a moving school bus while driving with Apple, 9, on the back.

    The driver of the heavy vehicle appeared to brake after spotting the pair, avoiding a collision by mere seconds.

    Her husband Chris Martin, 36, and son Moses, 7, were trailing behind on another vespa.

    Lucky escape: Gwyneth Paltrow pulled out in front of a school bus while driving a scooter in LA with daughter Apple on the back on Wednesday

    Frightening moment: She avoided a collision but just mere seconds

    Unscathed: The actress appeared unaware of the near miss

     

    The Goop blogger appeared oblivious and continued riding on.

    She drove off with her husband Chris behind and pulled up next to cars at traffic lights. Mail Online has contacted a spokesperson for Gwyneth but did not receive an immediate response.

    The couple were making the most of the warm weather, choosing to ride bikes rather than take a car for the family outing.

    Sunshine ride: The blonde beauty was driving solo earlier in the day

    Hold on Apple! The Goop blogger moved to LA recently so her children could experience a slower lifestyle

    Two for the road: The actress was joined by her husband by Chris Martin

    Hitting the road: Chris pulls ahead of his Mrs. in thick LA traffic

    Clad in a light grey top with shorts and beige sandals, she had summer written all over her.

    Along with a white helmet which matched her white Vespa, Gwyneth had on black gloves.

    Chris was more dressed down in blue slacks, multi-coloured trainers, and a dark grey T-shirt with the word SOHO written on it. He too sported a pair of gloves.

    Hold on tight: The Coldplay singer was joined by his son

    The actress opened up about her life in the June issue of Glamour UK, admitting that she's had some very tough times in her life.

    'It’s hard being married,' she confessed.

    'You go through great times, you go through terrible times. We’re the same as any couple.

    'I asked my dad once, “How did you and Mum stay married for 33 years?” And he said, “Well, we never wanted to get divorced at the same time.” And I think that’s what happens.

    'When two people throw in the towel at the same time, then you break up, but if one person’s saying, “Come on, we can do this,” you carry on.'

    Happy couple: Chris and Gwyneth have been married since 2003

    But the cover girl says she and her husband are 'good friends.'

    'I’m a very grounded, homey person and Chris is a very mad scientist, genius songwriter,' she said.

    'So I never say “Where are you? You should be home by now.” I never place demands on him because I think he’s a really talented man and he’s putting something good into the world.'

    The star also admitted that she has experienced a lot of suffering.

    'I've had a great deal of suffering in my life. I've lost people—my first cousin, my best friend in a car accident. I've had a lot of things happen to me that I don't talk about in the press.

    'I've had a lot of really heartbreaking, difficult things,' she continued. 'But all of those things have strengthened my resolve to make the best of my life.'

    Kendall Jenner's Corseted Croc-Embossed Trench Coat Is the Statement Outerwear You Need This Fall

    Kendall Jenner has once again found a statement coat that will live eternally on my fall mood board. While out and about in Paris yesterday, the supermodel showed out in one of her most glamorous off-duty Fashion Month looks yet.

    18 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Kendall Jenner has once again found a statement coat that will live eternally on my fall mood board.

    While out and about in Paris yesterday, the supermodel showed out in one of her most glamorous off-duty Fashion Month looks yet.

    Pulled from Bottega Veneta’s Fall/Winter 2023 collection, the rusty-orange trench coat featured a glossy croc-embossed material and a structured silhouette that cinched her at the waist, akin to the figure-hugging dimensions of a corset.

    She coordinated the eye-popping piece with black elements, including leather knee-high boots with her signature kitten heel, narrow rectangular shades, and leather gloves.

    BACKGRID

    She later praised the Bottega coat on her Instagram Story, writing, “this. Bottega. coat. lucky me.”

    Instagram

    This isn’t the first time Jenner has discovered subversive ways to style the otherwise classic trench.

    Earlier this week, she repped the Row in a pale khaki-green trench coat that was tucked into a high-rise black midi skirt. She accessorized the look with more pieces from the Row—including a thin black belt, a large leather tote bag, and loafers—as well as a pair of Gucci sunglasses.

    Claudio Lavenia//Getty Images

    Prior to that, the 818 founder appeared at Milan Fashion Week in head-to-toe Gucci. The centerpiece of her ensemble was a pale beige double-breasted trench coat worn as a minidress. To up the ante, she added a pop of color with a pair of pointed-toe slingbacks in a glossy crimson and a coordinating Jackie 1961 purse in cherry red. For a finishing touch, she added black rectangular shades and gold earrings.

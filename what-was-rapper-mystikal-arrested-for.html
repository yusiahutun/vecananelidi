    What Was Rapper Mystikal Arrested For?

    Mystikal was arrested in 2002 for the charges of sexual battery. He pleaded guilty to sexual assault charges in 2003 and served six years in prison for his crimes.

    He faced charges of rape and kidnapping charges in 2017. The rapper initially did not accept his involvement with the woman who had accused him of the changes.

    His case was dismissed later as the new evidence was presented to a second grand jury.

    What Is Mystikal's Real Name?

    Mystikal's real name is Michael Lawrence Tyler. He was born in New Orleans, Louisiana, on September 22, 1970.

    He went to Cohen High School. And to pursue his further studies, he joined the United States Army as a combat engineer. He lost his dad when he was seven years old. 

    The rapper's father used to run a small neighborhood store. His life began after he released his debut album, and he was an opening act for Run-DMC and Doug E. Fresh at an outdoor concert at the Treme Center.

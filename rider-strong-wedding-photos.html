    06 Feb 2024, 00:00

    It's "Boy Meets Bride" for Rider Strong.

    The "Boy Meets World" star -- and former teen idol -- tied the knot with longtime girlfriend Alexandra Barreto on Sunday ... and we've got brand new photos from their special day!

    In the exclusive shot above, the two are seen after saying their "I Dos." Barreto looked stunning in a Nicole Miller wedding dress while Strong was strapping in a suit from Rag & Bone.

    The two got married at a summer camp in Oregon and it was a camp-themed affair, with bunks, ropes courses, games and even a talent show.

    Strong also got his favorite band, Typhoon, to perform at the reception after he mentioned them on Twitter.

    In another new shot from the big day, Barreto can be seen with her bridesmaids -- including "The Following" star Natalie Zea.

    All the bridesmaids wore skirts that were handmade by Anjou Clothing on Esty, and each one wore their own chambray shirt, belt and boots.

    Rider wasn't the only "Boy Meets World" star who got married over the weekend either!

    His costar Danielle Fishel tied the knot on Saturday to Tim Belusko.

    And while she looked gorgeous in some of the photos that have hit the Internet, Fishel had to slam some critics who called her "fat."

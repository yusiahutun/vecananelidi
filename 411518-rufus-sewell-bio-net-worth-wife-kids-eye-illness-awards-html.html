    Rufus Sewell biography

    Sewell was born on October 29th, 1967, in Twickenham, Middlesex, England, in the United Kingdom. He is the son of William Sewell and Jo Sewell. His father was an Australian animator; he died when Rufus was 10, while his mother was a trained pianist and waitress. He has a brother called Caspar.

    How old is Rufus Sewell? The actor is currently 53 years. However, he will be celebrating his 54th birth anniversary on October 29th, 2021.

    Educational background

    Rufus began his studies at Trafalgar Junior School. He later joined Orleans Park School and then West Thames College. While at the college, a drama teacher, Judi Dench, identified him and sent him to audition for drama school. He was enrolled at the Central School of Speech and Drama in London.

    Profession

    Rufus began his career in 1991. He made his debut in the film Twenty-One, acting as Bobby - a young drug-addicted man. He played the role impressively and attracted numerous roles. Today, Rufus Sewell movies and TV shows are popular in showbiz.

    Stranger Things star Natalia Dyer looked chic at Fashion Week in Paris, with her look being miles away from her character from the hit TV show.

    The 28-year-old, who is still dating her Netflix co-star Charlie Heaton, oozed glamour and sophistication this week. Natalia Dyer, whose fame skyrocketed with Stranger Things, let her outfit do all the work in her recent fashion centric appearance. The TV actor attended the Saint Laurent Show during Paris Fashion Week alongside other stars.

    Natalia Dyer’s chic outfit is upside down compared to Stranger Things

    Taking a break from Stranger Things, Natalia Dyer wore a pinstripe midi skirt for the event. Pairing it with a halterneck blouse that had a large bow detailing on the front. The blouse draped behind her slim figure, and was a sheer material, for an edgy look.

    Her dark lips matched the gorgeous top, as well as black heels and gold jewelry to pull it all together.

    The celebrity’s sleek hair was straight and kept behind her ears, with the blonde locks cascading behind her shoulders.

    The look really showed off her toned legs and minimalist but bold outfit.

    It’s a far cry from her character in Stranger Things, where Natalia Dyer plays Nancy. The show, which is set in the 1980s, highlights bold fashion, bright colors and is totally kitsch. The authentic flavor of the show’s style stays true to the decade.

    Natalia is always chic

    The star is always pushing the boundaries when it comes to fashion. Her Instagram is full of various styles, whether it’s pantsuits, dresses or throwbacks to iconic outfits.

    She always creates a show-stopping moment, like at the Paris premiere for Stranger Things season 3. Natalia wore a red and ivory gown with off-the-shoulders short sleeves and matching heels to the event.

    For the 2018 Emmys, she chose to go full glam with a gold bead-detailed skintight dress that hugged her figure. The string straps and floor length gown oozed Old Hollywood, and her brunette hair was styled in tousled waves.

    Then at the SAG Awards Show the same year, she wore Dior’s Zodiac Gown and it was everything bit of magical.

    Natalia and Charlie’s dating timeline

    The best couple to emerge from the Upside Down, the pair met on the set of the show. Both Natalia Dyer and Charlie Heaton had supporting roles in the Stranger Things cast.

    Looking at their relationship timeline, it was back in September 2016 when Charlie called Natalia one of his “amigos”. It was also only a week later when she posted a picture of him on her Instagram. This is when fans began to wonder if they were more than just co-stars.

    Then in October they wore adorable matching costumes for Halloween. They, alongside two pals, dressed up as characters from The Wizard of Oz, a classic movie. Charlie was the Scarecrow, and Natalia dressed up as the Cowardly Lion.

    We fast-forward to January 2017 when the pair were seen leaving LAX together. It came after they attended the Golden Globe Awards. It’s also the same month when a source said they were dating, and the romance blossomed at the start of filming for season two.

    Then, a photo in October that year finally confirmed it. They were seen holding hands as they walked down a New York City street. In November, they were snapped kissing and enjoying PDA in London.

    The rest is just history – or purely romance.

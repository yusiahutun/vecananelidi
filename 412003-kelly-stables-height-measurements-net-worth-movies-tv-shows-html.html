    Kelly Stables biography

    The actress was born on 26th January 1978 in St Louis, Missouri, USA. She is the daughter of Jill Cissel (mother) but has not offered any information about her father. She has an elder sister, Tanya Childress.

    The model was raised in St Louis alongside her elder sister. She attended Lafayette High School, where she was a cheerleader and the prom queen. She was also elected as the class president during her senior high school year.

    After completing her high school studies, she joined the University of Missouri. She enrolled for a Degree in TV Communications and Theater Performance. The actress graduated in 1999.

    Kelly fell in love with acting from her childhood days. She played the role of the angel during a local Christmas play in her neighbourhood at the age of five.

    Career

    Kelly relocated from St Missouri to Los Angeles to pursue acting as her full-time career after graduating. She started by featuring in movies such as Spiderman and The Princess Diaries 2: Royal Engagement. Her breakthrough in the industry came while acting in the iconic horror movie, The Ring.

    There’s no feeling I love more than sinking into my king mattress after nine-plus hours on my feet. It’s like all the tension of the day melts away; a huge sense of relief rushes over me and a big, audible ahhhh is simply inevitable. While not much compares to that feeling (I know you’ve felt that, too), there is something similarly satisfying: slipping into cozy boots that feel like a hug for your feet. Pure ahhhhhh.

    It’s one of the reasons I get so excited when I open my weather app and see that it’s, wait for it, 40 degrees. Sure, call me crazy for getting excited about the chilly temperatures, but that’s partly because I grew up in Minnesota and partly because I really can’t stand the heat and humidity. But most of all, it’s because it means I can finally wear my shearling-lined boots. 

    Shearling boots, like Uggs, return like clockwork this time of year, and while some celebs prematurely wear them — I’m talking in August! — most start to pull out the tall suede boots come November. Uggs are a classic, but there’s also its sister brand Koolaburra that has similar styles for much less — and I found a pair starting at $30 that looks just like those famous Uggs practically every celeb owns. Trust me when I say you’ll be wearing them nonstop this time of year.

    Koolaburra by Ugg Aribel Short Mid-Calf Boot

    Koolburra is one of my best-kept fashion secrets, because you get Ugg-like quality at prices that you would rarely (if ever) find for real Uggs. The styles are also similar to tried-and-true Ugg options, but these mid-calf boots are particularly of interest because they look nearly identical to the tall style so many celebs have worn. They have the same brown suede outer, plus a plush, shearling-lined interior that feels like you're walking on clouds (aka, soft, fluffy, and light-as-air). Doesn’t that sound magical? Not to mention, it’ll keep your feet nice and toasty, even if you wear them sans socks.

    The boots also have a trendy, practical update. Yes, I’m talking about the side zipper detailing that adds an edgy touch to an otherwise simple, mid-calf silhouette, but it also makes the shoe easy to take on and off. The mid height is also ideal for areas that get lots of snow (ahem, like Minnesota), and makes for easy tucking. Yes, I still tuck my jeans into boots! 

    Koolaburra by Ugg Aribel Short Mid-Calf Boot

    The best part? Ahead of Amazon’s October Prime Day, the boots start at a reasonable $30 — 40 percent off their usual $100 price tag. If you need more convincing, let people who’ve worn them IRL offer some inspiration: “[They’re] very cozy for work and errands,” wrote one shopper, while another mentioned, “The fur is soft! What you would expect an authentic Ugg to be! Five stars!” I’m sold. 

    Shop the winter boots you’ll be living in for the next six months ASAP while they’re on sale for as little as $30. Nearly every size is still available, but probably not for long.

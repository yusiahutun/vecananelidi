    Best for energising

    Necessaire The Body Exfoliator

    Best for energising

    Necessaire The Body Exfoliator

    Credit: Sephora

    Nécessaire is known for its beautifully scented body products, and this jelly-like exfoliator is the star of the line-up. It's generously blended with fine pumice stone alongside resurfacing glycolic acid, and imparts the brand's famously invigorating eucalyptus scent. A spa-standard treat that delivers real results.

    Best for a botanical scent

    Aesop Geranium Leaf Body Scrub

    Best for a botanical scent

    Aesop Geranium Leaf Body Scrub

    Credit: Sephora

    Aesop's gel-textured body scrub smells as divine as any fan of the brand will know to expect. It contains both pumice and bamboo powder to deliver a thorough yet fine exfoliation, and boasts a fragrance that'll make you reluctant to leave the shower. A true sensory treat.

    Best body scrub for satisfying grit

    Beauty Pie Super Healthy Skin Nourishing Body Polish

    Best body scrub for satisfying grit

    Beauty Pie Super Healthy Skin Nourishing Body Polish

    Credit: Beauty Pie

    Formulated in Italy, Beauty Pie's indulgent body scrub ticks every box. The chunky salt flakes deliver a thoroughly satisfying texture, and fruit enzymes bolster the resurfacing powers. Coconut milk lends moisture, while orange and lemon essential oils make it smell, as the brand says, "subtle, citrussy, and expensive." We couldn't agree more.

    Best luxury body scrub

    Costa Brazil Body Scrub

    Best luxury body scrub

    Costa Brazil Body Scrub

    Credit: .

    Niche beauty at its best, Costa Brazil creates products that deserve to be saved for special occasions. This body scrub utilises the brand's star Amazonian plant extracts – murumuru and green coffee – in a base of hemi-squalane (last seen in your face serum) and moisture-grabbing glycerin. The thick jelly texture melts into a milk once you step in the shower, and leaves skin feeling hydrated, not stripped.

    Best body scrub for keratosis pilaris

    DERMAdoctor KP Duty Body Scrub

    Best body scrub for keratosis pilaris

    DERMAdoctor KP Duty Body Scrub

    Credit: Lookfantastic

    If you're looking to combat extremely dry, rough skin or a case of keratosis pilaris, this active scrub is the solution. It's not the most pampering of products, but the strong blend of exfoliating acids will slough away troublesome patches like no other. Massage it gently into dry skin before rinsing away.

    Best body scrub for very dry, rough skin

    Ameliorate Smoothing Body Exfoliant

    Best body scrub for very dry, rough skin

    Ameliorate Smoothing Body Exfoliant

    Credit: Lookfantastic

    Chemical and physical exfoliation combine in Ameliorate’s advanced scrub. Lactic acid gently dissolves dead skin cells and debris (freeing ingrown hairs in the process) while physical granules deliver that satisfying surface-level renewal. Use it head-to-toe, paying particular attention to the backs of your arms to keep keratosis pilaris (a bumpy build-up of keratin) at bay.

    Best body scrub for a spa experience

    Tata Harper Smoothing Body Scrub

    Best body scrub for a spa experience

    Tata Harper Smoothing Body Scrub

    Credit: Net a Porter

    This sensorial body scrub is one of the best ways to indulge in Tata Harper's beautifully scented, batch-blended body line. Made on the brand's Vermont farm from natural (and almost entirely organic) ingredients, it boasts a herbal scent and deeply hydrating argan-oil base. A great choice for gifting.

    Best for congestion and blackheads

    Kate Somerville ExfoliKate™ Resurfacing Body Scrub

    Best for congestion and blackheads

    Kate Somerville ExfoliKate™ Resurfacing Body Scrub

    Credit: Sephora

    With lactic and salicylic acids, Kate Somerville's body scrub is best placed to tackle congested skin, keratosis pilaris and body acne. The green-tinged paste smells slightly medicinal and has a generous sandy grit that sets to work on skin that needs a serious scrub.

    Best body scrub for softening

    Sunday Riley Charcoal Smoothie Jelly Body Scrub

    Best body scrub for softening

    Sunday Riley Charcoal Smoothie Jelly Body Scrub

    Credit: Cult Beauty

    If you find oil-based scrubs leave too much of a residue on your limbs, consider this innovative jelly-textured option from Sunday Riley. It's packed with charcoal for a thorough cleanse, salicylic acid for clarifying, and biodegradable wax particles that'll leave skin feeling softer than ever without scratching.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Best natural body scrub

    Susanne Kaufmann Refining Body Scrub

    Best natural body scrub

    Susanne Kaufmann Refining Body Scrub

    Credit: Space NK

    Susanne Kaufmann’s creamy, grainy scrub uses sand and pumice powder to really exfoliate dry skin. It’s especially satisfying on tired feet and scaly elbows.

    St John’s Wort and echinacea (both home-grown by the founder in the Austrian alps) work to soothe irritation and encourage blemishes to heal, while plant-based hyaluronic acid delivers lasting hydration.

    Best body scrub for fake-tanners

    St. Tropez Body Polish and Exfoliator

    Best body scrub for fake-tanners

    St. Tropez Body Polish and Exfoliator

    Credit: Lookfantastic

    Exfoliating your skin before fake-tanning is crucial, but you want to avoid an oil-based scrub, because it'll affect the way your tan develops. Bondi Sands has the solution: this coconut and powdered walnut scrub is completely oil-free (the texture is coarse and almost sandy), meaning it'll slough and smooth while prepping your skin for a fresh application of tan.

    Best AHA body scrub

    Comfort Zone Body Strategist Peel Scrub

    Best AHA body scrub

    Comfort Zone Body Strategist Peel Scrub

    Credit: Lookfantastic

    From the sister brand to beloved haircare line Davines, this double-duty body scrub contains both natural (and biodegradable) silica beads and poly-hydroxy-acids to ensure a seriously thorough exfoliation. For the best skin-smoothing results, massage it in circular motions onto dry skin for a minute or two before washing away. It's a good one to employ before fake tan, too.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    Best for pure indulgence

    Mauli Reawaken Himalayan Hand & Body Scrub

    Best for pure indulgence

    Mauli Reawaken Himalayan Hand & Body Scrub

    Credit: Space NK

    An ode to ancient Ayurveda, Mauli Rituals is a brand with treasured ingredients and mind-altering aromas at its heart. This decadent body scrub features both: the oil-rich blend contains mineral-rich Himalayan salt alongside argan and moringa oils, while sweet orange, jasmine sambac and sandalwood create a sweet, spicy and completely addictive scent. Save it for long, indulgent baths.

    Best body scrub for sensitive skin

    Biossance Squalane + Enzyme Sugar Body Scrub

    Best body scrub for sensitive skin

    Biossance Squalane + Enzyme Sugar Body Scrub

    Credit: Space NK

    With gentle sugar particles and pomegranate enzymes, this light body scrub is a good choice for anyone who typically finds exfoliators too rough.

    The brand's star squalane extract is produced using sugarcane (instead of the usual olive) which requires significantly less land and water to harvest. Not just an impressively low-impact ingredient, it's also a molecular match for the squalane found naturally in our skin, meaning it absorbs seamlessly and delivers hydration that lasts.

    Best body scrub for scalp and skin

    Ouai Scalp & Body Scrub

    Best body scrub for scalp and skin

    Ouai Scalp & Body Scrub

    Credit: Cult Beauty

    It’s not just neglected limbs that can benefit from a good scrub – your scalp will appreciate the deep-cleansing benefits, too. Powered by coconut-derived foaming agents (rather than SLS), Ouai’s scrub whisks away product build up and trapped sebum from your roots, and contains a dose of balancing probiotics to restore harmony, too. Plus, it’s incredibly satisfying to use at the end of a long week.

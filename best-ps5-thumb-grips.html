    One of the biggest gripes for console gamers is the limitations of controllers in terms of movement and precision gameplay, versus the silky smooth tracking that keyboard and mouse gameplay can provide.     FAQ

    Q: Do Gaming Thumb Grips Actually Help?

    Yes. Thumb grips can not only save your thumbs and your thumbsticks, but they can even give you a bit of an advantage over your regular thumbstick coating.

    Many are textured or made from grippier plastic, keeping your thumbs exactly where they're supposed to be during gaming sessions.

    Q: What Are Thumb Grips Made From?

    Usually, they are made from high-quality silicone. They offer protection to your regular joysticks, as well as providing an improved, grippier surface for your thumbs.

    30 Mar 2024, 00:00

    A warm happy birthday to everyone who is celebrating today (2 May). Here at HELLO!, we are taking a look at some of the famous faces who will be toasting their birthdays with their loved ones. From Britain's very own little Princess to a footballing legend to a Hollywood star, see who are celebrating today:

    Princess Charlotte turns three

    Princess Charlotte has turned three! The adorable daughter of the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge has had a busy year – she began nursery school for the very first time back in January, and welcomed a brand new baby brother, Prince Louis, in April. The little Princess also made history this year at the first female British royal not to lose her place in line to the throne to a younger male sibling. Not bad for a three year old!

    Princess Charlotte turned three

    David Beckham turns 43

    Many happy returns David! The retired footballer has had a busy year by achieving a long-term goal of creating a Miami football team while being a hands on dad with his four children – Brooklyn, Romeo, Cruz and Harper. In fact, the Beckham children congratulated their dad on his new venture in a sweet Instagram video, where Harper said: "Hi Daddy! I can't wait to see your first football match."

    David turned 43

    Dwayne Johnson turns 46

    The Rock has turned 46! Dwayne has had a fantastic year, and welcomed a baby girl named Tiana Gia with his girlfriend, Lauren Hashian, on 17 April. Sharing a photo of himself with his newborn daughter, he wrote: "To my third and youngest daughter, Tiana Gia - like I did when your two older sisters Simone Alexandra and Jasmine Lia were born, you have my word, I’ll love, protect, guide and make ya laugh for the rest of my life." The dad-of-three also saw success with the Jumanji reboot, Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle, where he plays an awkward teenage boy trapped in the body of 'Dr. Smolder Bravestone'.  

    Dwayne turned 46

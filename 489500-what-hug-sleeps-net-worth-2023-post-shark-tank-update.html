    What is a Hug Sleep pod?

    It is a comfortable swaddle for adults founded by Matt Mundt and Angie Kupper. Matt wanted to try the weighted blankets immediately after they became a trend. Initially, their pressure was comforting, but later, he discovered that the weighted blankets trapped a lot of heat. In addition, he found they were hard to wash.

    As a result, he thought of designing a more comfortable blanket. Therefore, they opted to use compression instead of weight to apply deep-touch pressure technology. Matt and his wife thus created an adult swaddle enough to wrap a person’s entire body. It was called the Sleep Pod and was launched in 2019 under Hug Sleep.

    Who are the owners of Hug Sleep?

    The product is owned by a husband-wife duo, Matt Mundt and Angie Kupper. The lovely couple hails from Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Mundt has a degree in mechanical engineering, while her wife has a master's degree in community psychology.

    After Angie’s mother committed suicide, she became passionate about helping people struggling with different mental illnesses. Therefore, after completing her studies, she worked as a therapist in the mental health industry for over a decade.

    On the other hand, her husband began working as a product designer for several renowned companies in the mechanical engineering sector.

    Katie Holmes made her closing argument for this latest New York Fashion Week in a look that flawlessly championed a major trend of the moment: lingerie as outside clothing.

    The Dawson’s Creek alum sat front row at Michael Kors’s Fall 2024 runway show Tuesday in New York, next to fellow VIPs Blake Lively, Brie Larson, Rachel Brosnahan, Gabrielle Union, Meghann Fahy, and Nina Dobrev.

    For the occasion, Holmes pulled out a sexy day-to-night look, composed of a totally see-through black lace maxi dress paired with a sleek black blazer, and layered over matching lingerie. The dress featured a pleated skirt with subtle ruffled layers, and a scrunchy built-in belt that cinched at the waist.

    Gotham//Getty Images

    Holmes wore a dainty lacy bralette to match the dress, as well as matte black high-waisted panties. She added an extra layer of sheer black with her tights, which she paired with strappy Wite-Out white heels. She also carried a star-shaped white oval clutch with a sculptural golden clasp.

    Her golden surrealist earrings matched her clutch, and Holmes finished the elegant look with a curly hairdo, natural makeup, and a bright red lip.

    Michael Ostuni//Getty Images

    Holmes has been showing off her versatile style this Fashion Week. On Super Bowl Sunday, she wore an angelic white satin dress to sit front row at Ulla Johnson’s Fall/Winter 2024 show—a designer she regularly supports. Before that, on Saturday, Holmes stepped out in a full blue denim look styled with electric-pink tights to attend Alice + Olivia’s show.

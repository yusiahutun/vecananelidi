Key Takeaways PSU shrouds are covers designed to conceal the power supply unit and other unsightly accessories in a PC case, improving the overall appearance and cable management. They reduce dust accumulation inside the case, improving airflow and preventing overheating issues. PSU shrouds offer additional expansion options, such as mounting slots for SSDs and case fans, as well as support for water cooling radiators and pump/reservoir combos.

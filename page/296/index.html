Romantic Jane Eyre musical to open in Moline

06 Feb 2024, 00:00

It’s all in the family (in some key ways) for the new Black Box Theatre production of “Jane Eyre: The Musical.” Making its area premiere, opening Friday, March 15, the classic story has music and lyrics by Paul Gordon and a book by John Caird, based on the 1847 novel by Charlotte Brontë. The musical premiered on Broadway in 2000. The Tony-nominated musical brings Brontë’s classic gothic romance to life as the audience follows the independent, passionate governess Jane Eyre (played by Kiera Lynn Martin in her BBT debut), through her harsh childhood after being left as an orphan to an uncaring aunt, through her employment as a governess at Thornfield Hall.

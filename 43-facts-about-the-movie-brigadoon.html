    Who are the main characters in "Brigadoon"?
    The main characters in "Brigadoon" are Tommy Albright, Jeff Douglas, Fiona MacLaren, and Meg Brockie. Tommy and Jeff stumble upon the enchanting village of Brigadoon during their travels, and their encounters with the villagers lead to a series of magical and heartwarming events.
